## Purpose

This document defines **how Marketplate expresses its brand in market** — campaigns, content, PR, social, and community building. It translates [Brand Strategy](../brand/brand-strategy.md) and [Positioning](../brand/positioning.md) into executable marketing programs for Phase 1 launch.

Every program must pass two filters: **Does it reinforce trust?** and **Would we be proud if a journalist described this tactic?** If either answer is no, the program does not ship.

## Brand Marketing Principles

| Principle | Source | Marketing application |
|-----------|--------|----------------------|
| **Trust is the product** | [Brand Strategy](../brand/brand-strategy.md) | Lead campaigns with verification proof, not convenience |
| **Creator brand forward** | [Positioning](../brand/positioning.md) | Chef names, stories, and kitchens — Marketplate recedes |
| **Calm, not hype** | [Voice — Calm](../brand/voice-and-tone.md#4-calm) | No countdown timers, flash sales, or fear-based copy |
| **Precise claims** | [Voice — Trustworthy](../brand/voice-and-tone.md#1-trustworthy) | Every trust claim verifiable; Legal + Trust review before publish |
| **Premium, not luxury** | [Brand Personality](../brand/brand-strategy.md#brand-personality) | Quality photography and copy — not exclusivity theater |
| **Human stories** | [Brand Strategy — Visual Direction](../brand/brand-strategy.md#visual--experiential-direction) | Real creators, real kitchens — not stock food imagery |

## Marketing Guardrails

### What we do

| Tactic | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Creator spotlight campaigns | "Meet the verified chefs of [Launch Market]" |
| Verification education | "What 'Verified Kitchen' means on Marketplate" |
| Behind-the-kitchen content | Production process, sourcing stories, compliance journey |
| Local food ecosystem celebration | Partnerships with kitchens, markets, food orgs |
| Editorial collections | Curated discovery — [Marketplate Collections](#marketplate-collections) |
| Trust-forward onboarding content | Creator and buyer education aligned with Help Center |

### What we do not do (dark patterns and trust erosion)

These tactics are **explicitly prohibited** regardless of short-term conversion lift.

| Prohibited tactic | Why | Alternative |
|-------------------|-----|-------------|
| **Fake urgency** | "Only 2 left!" when inventory is not constrained | Real cutoff times: "Order by Thursday 6 PM for this week's menu" |
| **Fake scarcity** | Countdown timers on non-expiring offers | Transparent availability from creator capacity |
| **Hidden fees** | Service fees revealed at final checkout step | Full cost visible before payment — [Voice — Precise](../brand/voice-and-tone.md#3-precise) |
| **Verification theater** | Badges or copy implying verification that doesn't exist | Only display credentials actually earned |
| **Bait-and-switch discovery** | Promoted listing that doesn't match search intent | Trust-weighted ranking — [Discovery Ranking](../ai/discovery-ranking.md) |
| **Misleading photography** | Stock images presented as creator food | Creator-submitted, moderation-reviewed photos only |
| **Discount-first acquisition** | "50% off first order" as primary message | Trust and creator story; discounts only if strategic and transparent |
| **Engagement bait** | "Tag 5 friends to win!" | Community content that serves creator–customer relationships |
| **Pre-checked marketing consent** | Opt-in boxes checked by default | Explicit consent per [Legal](../legal/) |
| **Drip campaign pressure** | "Last chance!!!" email series | Calm, informative sequences — [Education Playbooks](../docs/customer-success/education-playbooks.md) |
| **Obscured seller identity** | Platform brand replaces creator in ads | Creator name and photo in all marketplace advertising |
| **Review manipulation** | Incentivized or fake reviews | Verified-purchase reviews only |
| **Auto-renewal obscurity** | Hidden subscription terms | Clear terms before commitment (Phase 8+) |

→ Brand guardrail: [Brand Strategy — Never sacrifice trust for growth](../brand/brand-strategy.md#brand-guardrails)  
→ Product guardrails: [Value Propositions — Messaging Guardrails](../product/value-props.md#messaging-guardrails)

### Pre-publish checklist

Before any external campaign ships:

- [ ] Trust claims verified with Trust & Safety
- [ ] Creator names and photos have written approval
- [ ] Pricing and fee references match current policy (or marked TBD — not invented)
- [ ] No prohibited tactics from table above
- [ ] Copy reviewed against [Voice Checklist](../brand/voice-and-tone.md#voice-checklist)
- [ ] Landing page has sufficient supply (no empty browse)
- [ ] Legal review for jurisdiction-specific claims in `[Launch Market]`

## Campaign Framework

### Campaign types (Phase 1)

| Campaign type | Objective | Timing | Primary audience |
|---------------|-----------|--------|------------------|
| **Founding creator** | Recruit and celebrate launch cohort | Pre-launch → soft launch | Creators |
| **Verification education** | Reduce verification anxiety; increase completion | Pre-launch → ongoing | Creators |
| **Creator spotlight** | Build trust through real stories | Soft launch → ongoing | Customers + creators |
| **Local launch** | Announce public availability in `[Launch Market]` | Public launch | Customers |
| **Collection launch** | Editorial discovery moments | Public launch → ongoing | Customers |
| **Seasonal / cultural** | Relevant food moments (holidays, game day) | Ongoing | Customers |

| Element | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|

| **Audience** | Persona-specific — see [Personas](../product/personas.md) |

| **Measurement** | Primary + guardrail metrics defined before launch |
| **Trust review** | Trust & Safety sign-off on claims |

## Content Strategy

### Content mission

Help independent food entrepreneurs build trusted businesses — and help customers discover verified local food with confidence.

Content is **education-first**, not lead-gen spam. Every piece should be useful if the reader never signs up.

### Content pillars

| Pillar | Audience | Topics | Trust alignment |
|--------|----------|--------|-----------------|
| **Trust & verification** | Creators | Identity verification, kitchen standards, cottage food compliance | Core thesis |
| **Creator business** | Creators | Menu pricing, photography, capacity planning, share-link strategy | Software value prop |
| **Local food stories** | Both | Creator profiles, cuisine spotlights, behind-the-kitchen | Community value prop |
| **Buyer confidence** | Customers | How verification works, allergen safety, what to expect | Trust value prop |
| **Marketplace guides** | Both | How to order, pickup etiquette, review guidelines | Product education |

### Content formats

| Format | Channel | Cadence (Phase 1) | Owner |
|--------|---------|-------------------|-------|
| **Creator spotlight (long-form)** | Blog, email | 2× / month | Marketing |
| **Verification guides** | Blog, Help Center | As needed per jurisdiction | Marketing + Trust |
| **Short-form video (behind-the-kitchen)** | Instagram, TikTok | 1× / week | Marketing + Creators |
| **Newsletter (creator)** | Email | Bi-weekly | Marketing + CS |
| **Newsletter (buyer)** | Email | Monthly (post soft launch) | Marketing |
| **SEO articles** | Blog | 2× / month | Marketing |
| **Collection editorial copy** | Product surface | Per collection | Marketing + Product |

### SEO strategy

| Audience | Keyword intent | Example topics |
|----------|---------------|----------------|
| **Creators** | Business setup, compliance | "How to start a cottage food business in [state]" |
| **Customers** | Trust-seeking discovery | "Verified meal prep [Launch Market]," "local baker [Launch Market]" |

**Avoid targeting:** "Cheap food delivery," "fastest delivery," "restaurant deals" — wrong category and buyer intent.

→ Channels: [Acquisition Channels — SEO](acquisition-channels.md#creator-facing-content--seo)

### Content × lifecycle alignment

Content supports [Customer Lifecycle](../docs/customer-success/customer-lifecycle.md) stages:

| Lifecycle stage | Content type | Example |
|-----------------|--------------|---------|
| Creator Prospect | Trust education | "Why verification helps you sell more" |
| Creator Applicant | Verification guides | "Kitchen photo checklist" |
| Creator Activated | OS tips | "Share your storefront link effectively" |
| Buyer Visitor | Trust education | "What verified creator means" |
| Buyer First Order | Expectation setting | "Your first pickup: what to expect" |
| Buyer Repeat | Discovery | "New creators this week in [Launch Market]" |

→ Email sequences: [Education Playbooks](../docs/customer-success/education-playbooks.md)

## Creator Co-Marketing

Creators are co-marketers — their brand leads, platform amplifies.

### Co-marketing programs

| Program | Description | Creator approval required |
|---------|-------------|--------------------------|
| **Launch spotlight** | Featured creator story on blog, social, email | Yes — written consent |
| **Collection feature** | Creator dishes in editorial collection | Yes |
| **PR inclusion** | Creator quoted in launch press | Yes |
| **Photography support** | Platform-funded menu photo session (select creators) | Yes + usage rights |
| **Founding creator badge** | Non-pay-to-win recognition on profile | Automatic per criteria |

### Creator marketing asset kit

Provide creators with share-ready assets via creator dashboard:

| Asset | Format | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Storefront link + QR code | Digital | Primary acquisition tool |
| Social post templates | Instagram, Facebook | Customizable; creator brand forward |
| Verification badge graphics | PNG | Only for earned verification tiers |
| "Find me on Marketplate" copy | Text | Pre-written, editable |
| Story highlights guide | PDF / in-app | How to add storefront link to bio |

**Guardrail:** Assets must not imply Marketplate endorsement of food quality beyond verification — see [Help Center — Trust](../docs/help-center/trust.md).

## PR & Earned Media

### PR narrative

**Primary story:** Marketplate is infrastructure for the independent food economy — verified creators, not another delivery app.

| Angle | Audience | Key proof points |
|-------|----------|------------------|
| **Category creation** | Business/tech press | Trust thesis, creator OS, market gap |
| **Local food economy** | Local press, food media | Founding creators, job creation, cottage food enablement |
| **Trust & safety** | Food safety / consumer press | Verification stack, compliance support |
| **Creator empowerment** | Lifestyle / food press | Creator stories, business tools |

### Anti-angles (do not pitch)

- "Uber Eats killer" or delivery speed comparisons
- "Anyone can sell food from home" (verification is the story)
- Unverified launch metrics or inflated GMV

### PR timeline

| Phase | PR activity |
|-------|---------------|
| **Pre-launch** | Brief key food writers; embargoed creator profiles prepared |
| **Soft launch** | Local food media outreach; creator story placements |
| **Public launch** | Press release, founder interviews, collection launches |
| **Ongoing** | Creator milestones, trust reports (future), seasonal collections |

### Spokesperson guidelines

| Context | Spokesperson | Message discipline |
|---------|--------------|-------------------|
| Company vision | Founder / CEO | Category, trust thesis, long-term vision |
| Creator stories | Creator (preferred) or Founder | Creator voice first |
| Trust & verification | Trust lead or Founder | Factual, no approval time guarantees |
| Product details | Product lead | Feature accuracy; no roadmap promises |

→ Voice: [Positioning by Context — Press](../brand/positioning.md#positioning-by-context)

## Community Building

Community serves **commerce and trust** — not engagement for its own sake. Aligns with [Company Phases — Phase 7](../roadmap/company-phases.md#phase-7--community) vision, implemented lightly in Phase 1.

### Phase 1 community tactics

| Tactic | Platform | Purpose |
|--------|----------|---------|
| **Creator community channel** | Private group (Slack/Discord) | Feedback, peer support, launch coordination |
| **Local food events** | In-person | Creator meetups, kitchen tours, market partnerships |
| **Social presence** | Instagram, TikTok | Creator spotlights, behind-the-kitchen, verification education |
| **Newsletter community** | Email | Curated updates, new creators, collections |

### Community guardrails

| Rule | Rationale |
|------|-----------|
| No public community without moderation plan | Food safety and trust risks |
| Creator community is not a support channel | Direct to Help Center and Support |
| No platform-endorsed recipe sharing without licensing (Phase 4+) | IP and safety |
| Celebrate creators, not platform vanity metrics | Brand personality |
| Community features must strengthen creator–customer relationships | [Product Overview — What we are not](../product/overview.md#what-marketplate-is-not) |

→ Help Center community standards: [Community Guidelines](../docs/help-center/community-guidelines.md)

## Social Media Strategy

### Platform priorities (Phase 1)

| Platform | Priority | Content type |
|----------|----------|--------------|
| **Instagram** | Primary | Creator spotlights, food photography, verification education |
| **TikTok** | Secondary | Behind-the-kitchen short video, creator personality |
| **LinkedIn** | Secondary | Creator business stories, company milestones, partnerships |
| **X / Twitter** | Tertiary | Launch announcements, customer service routing |
| **Facebook** | Tertiary | Local community groups, creator share links |

### Social voice

Follow [Voice — Marketing & Growth](../brand/voice-and-tone.md#marketing--growth):

- Inspiring, confident, story-driven — still calm
- Lead with trust or creator identity in headlines
- Celebrate creators; share behind-the-kitchen content
- Single clear CTA per post

### Social do / don't

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Repost creator content (with permission) | Repost without credit or consent |
| Explain verification badges | Use verification language loosely |
| Show real kitchens and operators | Use stock food photography |
| Respond to questions with Help Center links | Provide food safety advice |
| Acknowledge mistakes plainly | Delete negative comments to hide issues |

## Measurement

Brand marketing reports against trust-aware metrics — not vanity alone.

| Metric | Type | Target direction |
|--------|------|------------------|
| **Brand search volume** | Awareness | ↑ ("Marketplate" + "[Launch Market]") |
| **Content → application rate** | Creator pipeline | ↑ |
| **Content → registration rate** | Customer pipeline | ↑ |
| **Email unsubscribe rate** | Quality | ↓ (low is good; high may signal spam) |
| **Social engagement rate** | Quality | Monitor — engagement alone is not success |
| **Share link adoption (creators)** | Activation | ↑ |
| **Trust-related support tickets from campaigns** | Guardrail | ↓ |
| **NPS (creator + buyer)** | Brand health | ↑ |
| **PR coverage sentiment** | Brand health | Neutral to positive; no trust controversies |

→ Brand metrics: [Brand Strategy — Success Metrics](../brand/brand-strategy.md#success-metrics-brand)  
→ CS metrics: [Success Metrics](../docs/customer-success/success-metrics.md)
