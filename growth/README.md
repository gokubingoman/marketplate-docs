## Purpose

Growth at Marketplate is not growth-at-all-costs user acquisition. We build **marketplace liquidity** through verified supply, creator-led demand, and trust-forward brand expression. Success is measured by [Verified GMV](../product/success-metrics-overview.md#north-star-metric) and its guardrails — not raw signups, discount-driven transactions, or unverified listing volume.

This folder is the **internal operating system** for Growth and Marketing. Customer Success owns post-acquisition lifecycle — see [Customer Success](../docs/customer-success/). Brand expression guidelines live in [brand/](../brand/). Product strategy lives in [product/](../product/).

## Core Principles

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Trust first** | Never accelerate acquisition by skipping verification, obscuring seller identity, or implying credentials that do not exist |
| **Supply before demand** | Verified creators and catalog depth precede broad customer marketing — empty discovery erodes brand |
| **Creator-led demand** | Creators bring their existing audiences; platform marketing amplifies, not replaces, creator relationships |
| **Quality over volume** | Prefer repeat purchase rate and creator retention over one-time discount hunters |
| **No dark patterns** | No fake urgency, hidden fees, verification theater, or engagement bait — see [Brand Marketing — Guardrails](brand-marketing.md#marketing-guardrails) |
| **Persona-aware** | A cottage food operator and a meal prep business require different acquisition messaging — see [Personas](../product/personas.md) |
| **Value-aligned messaging** | Lead with the value dimension that matters per audience — see [Value Propositions](../product/value-props.md) |

→ Brand guardrail: [Brand Strategy — Never sacrifice trust for growth](../brand/brand-strategy.md#brand-guardrails)

## Key Metrics

Growth reports against product and CS metrics — does not invent parallel definitions.

| Metric category | Primary metrics | Owner |
|-----------------|-----------------|-------|
| **North star inputs** | Verified GMV, active verified creators with orders, repeat customer rate | Product · Analytics |
| **Acquisition** | Creator application rate, verification completion rate, creator-attributed customer acquisition | Growth · CS |
| **Efficiency** | Blended CAC, creator CAC payback, channel ROI | Growth · Finance |
| **Brand health** | NPS, trust-related support tickets, brand sentiment | Marketing · CS |
| **Trust guardrails** | Trust incident rate, dispute rate, unverified listing attempts blocked | Trust · CS |
