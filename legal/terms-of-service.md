## 2. Platform Role & Marketplace Model

### 2.1 Marketplace facilitator

Marketplate operates a **verified supply marketplace** that connects independent food creators with customers. Marketplate provides software, verification infrastructure, payment facilitation, discovery, messaging, and dispute mediation.

Marketplate is **not** the seller of food products listed on the Platform and is **not** a restaurant, food producer, delivery company, or insurer of food quality.

Strategic model: [Marketplace Mechanics — Marketplace Model](../product/marketplace-mechanics.md#marketplace-model-overview)

### 2.2 Creator as merchant of record

Each creator who sells on the Platform is the **merchant of record** for their transactions. The creator:

- Sets prices, menus, and fulfillment terms
- Prepares and provides food products
- Is responsible for food safety, labeling, licensing, and compliance with applicable law
- Bears primary responsibility for order fulfillment and customer satisfaction

Marketplate facilitates payment collection and remittance to creators subject to the [Creator Agreement](creator-agreement.md) and applicable fees.

Payment model: [Marketplace Mechanics — Payment model](../product/marketplace-mechanics.md#payment-model)

### 2.3 No guarantee of outcomes

Marketplate verifies documentation and enforces platform standards but **does not guarantee**:

- Food quality, taste, or suitability for any dietary need
- Allergen-free preparation or absence of cross-contact
- Uninterrupted Platform availability
- Creator performance or continued operation

Kitchen verification is not a health inspection substitute. See [Food Commerce Compliance](food-commerce-compliance.md).
