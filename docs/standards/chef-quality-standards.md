# Chef Quality Standards

> Creator-side quality requirements for independent food entrepreneurs on Marketplate. These standards protect customers, preserve marketplace trust, and help creators build durable reputations.

**Status:** Active  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last updated:** 2026-07-03  
**Owner:** Creator Success · Trust & Safety  
**Governing authority:** [Marketplate Standards](marketplate-standards.md) · [Founding Constitution](../../company/constitution.md)

## Purpose

Marketplate exists so independent chefs can build **trusted businesses**. Creator quality is not optional polish — it is the supply-side foundation of the trust thesis.

This document defines what every creator must maintain to sell on Marketplate: kitchen conditions, food preparation, storage, temperature control, packaging, labeling, allergen accuracy, photography, menus, communication, fulfillment, and continuous improvement.

**Enforcement:** Quality failures trigger the [Trust enforcement ladder](../../product/marketplace-mechanics.md#trust-enforcement-ladder). Severe or repeated violations lead to suspension. Full framework: [Trust & Safety Standards](trust-and-safety-standards.md).

**Audience:** Creators (primary), Creator Success, Trust & Safety, Support, Product.

---

## Scope and Applicability

These standards apply to **all creators** accepting paid orders on Marketplate, regardless of persona:

| Persona | Additional context |
|---------|-------------------|
| Cottage food operators | [Marketplace Mechanics — Compliance](../../product/marketplace-mechanics.md#compliance-verification) |
| Commercial kitchen tenants | Multi-tenant kitchen linkage |
| Food trucks | Commissary + unit identification |
| Caterers, bakers, meal prep | Batch production and lead times |

Jurisdiction-specific rules supersede general guidance where stricter. Creators are responsible for knowing local regulations; Marketplate enforces platform minimums and documented compliance.

## Quality Philosophy

| Principle | Meaning for creators |
|-----------|---------------------|
| **Consistency** | Customers receive what the menu promises — every order, every time |
| **Transparency** | Disclose ingredients, allergens, and fulfillment honestly |
| **Professionalism** | Treat every order as a representation of your business |
| **Safety first** | When in doubt, do not ship — contact the customer and platform |
| **Continuous improvement** | Reviews and metrics are feedback, not punishment |

Quality and [Trust Score](trust-and-safety-standards.md#trust-score) are linked. High-quality creators earn visibility; chronic quality issues reduce discovery eligibility.

## Kitchen Cleanliness

The production environment must meet standards appropriate for commercial food preparation in the creator's jurisdiction.

### Minimum requirements

| Area | Standard |
|------|----------|
| **Surfaces** | Clean, sanitized food-contact and prep surfaces before each production session |
| **Hand washing** | Accessible hand-washing facility; hands washed before food handling and after contamination risk |
| **Equipment** | Clean, functional, and appropriate for the food type produced |
| **Pest control** | No evidence of pest activity in food production or storage areas |
| **Waste** | Prompt removal of food waste and garbage from prep areas |
| **Personal hygiene** | Clean attire; hair restrained; no jewelry that contaminates food on hands/arms |
| **Illness** | Do not prepare food while experiencing symptoms of communicable illness (vomiting, diarrhea, jaundice, sore throat with fever, open wounds on hands) |

### Verification alignment

Kitchen verification confirms the production environment meets platform standards — [Trust & Safety Standards — Kitchen Verification](trust-and-safety-standards.md#kitchen-verification). Material changes to the kitchen (relocation, new equipment layout, change of facility) require re-verification before continued sale.

### Violations

| Severity | Example | Typical response |
|----------|---------|------------------|
| **Critical** | Active pest infestation in verified kitchen photos or confirmed report | Immediate order suspension; re-verification required |
| **High** | Repeated customer reports of unsanitary packaging or foreign objects | Investigation; warning or suspension |
| **Medium** | Kitchen photos no longer match current environment | Re-verification request |

## Food Preparation

Preparation standards ensure food is made safely, consistently, and as described.

### Preparation requirements

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Approved recipes** | Prepare items per listed ingredients and methods; document changes before selling updated version |
| **Cross-contamination prevention** | Separate raw and ready-to-eat foods; dedicated utensils and surfaces where required |
| **Batch integrity** | Production runs match stated capacity and lead times — no overselling |
| **Custom orders** | Confirm specifications in writing (in-app messaging) before production starts |
| **Prohibited items** | Do not list or prepare items prohibited for your license type or jurisdiction |
| **Third-party ingredients** | Use reputable suppliers; retain receipts for traceability on request |

### Lead times and capacity

| Rule | Standard |
|------|----------|
| **Minimum lead time** | Honor stated lead times; do not accept orders you cannot fulfill on time |
| **Capacity limits** | Respect max orders per window/day configured in [Availability](../../pages/creator/availability.md) |
| **Sold out** | Mark items unavailable — do not accept orders for items you cannot produce |

Overselling is a platform defect when capacity gates fail; creators who routinely exceed capacity face [Trust Score](trust-and-safety-standards.md#trust-score) penalties.

## Storage

Proper storage prevents spoilage, cross-contamination, and quality degradation.

### Storage requirements

| Category | Standard |
|----------|----------|
| **Dry storage** | Cool, dry, off floor, protected from pests; FIFO rotation |
| **Refrigerated items** | ≤ 40°F (4°C) unless jurisdiction specifies otherwise |
| **Frozen items** | ≤ 0°F (−18°C); no refreeze after thaw |
| **Hot holding** | ≥ 135°F (57°C) when holding hot food before pickup/delivery |
| **Separation** | Raw proteins stored below ready-to-eat foods; allergens stored to prevent cross-contact where feasible |
| **Labeling (internal)** | Date-label prepared items and ingredients with prep date and use-by guidance |
| **Duration limits** | Do not sell food held beyond safe time/temperature limits for the product type |

Cottage food operators must respect category-specific storage rules for their jurisdiction — [Compliance verification](../../product/marketplace-mechanics.md#compliance-verification).

## Temperature Control

Temperature is a critical control point for perishable food.

### Standards by stage

| Stage | Requirement |
|-------|-------------|
| **Cooking** | Reach safe internal temperatures per food type and local code |
| **Cooling** | Rapid cool when required; never cool at room temperature beyond safe limits |
| **Hold before pickup** | Maintain hot or cold chain until customer handoff |
| **Pickup window** | Food ready within stated window; not hours early at unsafe temps |
| **Delivery** | Insulated bags or containers; minimize transit time within stated zone |
| **Customer handoff** | Communicate if item requires immediate refrigeration or reheating |

### Temperature violations

Confirmed sale of food held at unsafe temperatures is a **critical violation** — immediate suspension pending investigation.

Creators should maintain calibrated thermometers and document critical control points for batch operations.

## Packaging

Packaging protects food integrity and communicates professionalism. Platform principles: [Marketplate Standards — Packaging](marketplate-standards.md#packaging-standards).

### Creator packaging requirements

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Food-safe materials** | FDA-food-grade or equivalent containers, wraps, and liners |
| **Appropriate for hold time** | Packaging suitable for duration between completion and customer consumption |
| **Sealing** | Secure closure; leak-resistant for liquids and sauces |
| **Tamper evidence** | Sealed or stickered containers for pickup and delivery |
| **Temperature retention** | Insulated bags for hot/cold items on delivery or extended pickup windows |
| **Portion accuracy** | Package matches ordered quantity and size |
| **Exterior labeling** | Customer name or order ID; item name on multi-item orders |

### Prohibited packaging

- Non-food-grade containers (e.g., retail shopping bags for hot soup)
- Reused containers that cannot be sanitized
- Packaging that misstates contents or omits required allergen information

## Labeling

Labels carry legal and safety information. Accuracy is non-negotiable.

### Required label elements

| Element | When required |
|---------|---------------|
| **Item name** | All packaged orders |
| **Creator/business name** | All packaged orders |
| **Allergen summary** | All items with declared allergens — match listing exactly |
| **Order reference** | Pickup and delivery handoff |
| **Use-by / consume-by** | Perishable items when shelf life is limited |
| **Storage instructions** | "Refrigerate immediately," "Consume within X hours" when applicable |
| **Cottage food disclaimer** | Where required by jurisdiction |

### Listing-label alignment

What appears on the [Menu Item Editor](../../pages/creator/menu-item-editor.md) must match what is on the package. Material discrepancies are **allergen mislabeling** if allergens are affected — critical violation.

## Allergens and Dietary Information

Allergen accuracy is the highest-stakes creator obligation on Marketplate.

### Creator responsibilities

| Responsibility | Standard |
|----------------|----------|
| **Complete disclosure** | Declare all major allergens present in the item — per jurisdiction (US: milk, eggs, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, peanuts, wheat, soybeans, sesame) |
| **Cross-contact honesty** | Disclose shared equipment/facility cross-contact when applicable — "May contain traces of…" |
| **Dietary tags** | Only use "vegan," "gluten-free," etc. when accurate for the full preparation |
| **Updates** | Update listing before selling if recipe or supplier changes |
| **Customer inquiries** | Respond to allergen questions truthfully and promptly via [Messages](../../pages/creator/messages.md) |
| **Severe allergies** | Never guarantee allergen-free unless operation truly supports it; recommend against order if risk is unacceptable |

### Platform context

Customers acknowledge allergen information at checkout — [Transparency](../../product/marketplace-mechanics.md#transparency). Marketplate does not prepare food and cannot guarantee allergen-free preparation — [Help — Allergens](../../pages/customer/help.md).

### Violations

| Violation | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| **Confirmed allergen mislabeling** | Immediate suspension pending investigation |
| **Pattern of incomplete allergen fields** | Listing restriction until remediated |
| **False dietary claims** | Warning → suspension based on severity |

Full platform standards: [Marketplate Standards — Photography](marketplate-standards.md#photography-standards).

### Creator requirements

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|

## Menus and Listings

Menus are contracts with customers. Every field must be accurate and complete.

### Listing completeness standards

| Field | Requirement |
|-------|-------------|
| **Title** | Accurate, specific item name |
| **Description** | Ingredients, preparation method, portion size |
| **Price** | Correct; includes any required deposits for custom orders |
| **Allergens** | Complete mandatory allergen fields |
| **Dietary tags** | Only when accurate |
| **Photos** | Meet photography standards |
| **Fulfillment** | Valid pickup window, delivery zone, or event date |
| **Lead time** | Minimum lead time enforced for batch/custom items |
| **Availability** | Real-time; sold-out when capacity reached |

Incomplete listings cannot achieve full discovery eligibility — [Listing completeness rate](../../product/success-metrics-overview.md#transparency-compliance).

### Menu change discipline

- Update listings **before** selling changed recipes
- Pause or mark sold out during transitions
- Custom menu items require confirmed specifications in order notes

Catalog management: [Catalog](../../pages/creator/catalog.md) · [Menu Item Editor](../../pages/creator/menu-item-editor.md).

## Customer Communication

Professional, timely communication reduces disputes and builds repeat customers.

### Communication standards

| Context | Standard |
|---------|----------|
| **Order confirmation** | Acknowledge within 1 hour (or auto-accept per settings) |
| **Delay notification** | Notify customer proactively if order will miss stated window |
| **Pickup/delivery instructions** | Clear, specific — address, code, truck location, catering setup |
| **Allergen responses** | Reply within 4 hours during business hours |
| **Professional tone** | Follow [Voice and Tone](../../brand/voice-and-tone.md) — warm, precise, accountable |
| **Dispute engagement** | Respond to customer concerns before platform escalation when possible |

### Prohibited communication

- Off-platform payment requests
- Harassment, discrimination, or profanity toward customers
- Retaliation against reviewers
- Sharing customer contact info for marketing without consent
- False claims about verification or food safety credentials

Messaging surface: [Creator Messages](../../pages/creator/messages.md).

## Pickup Fulfillment

Pickup is the primary fulfillment model for many creators.

### Pickup standards

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Window accuracy** | Food ready within stated pickup window |
| **Location accuracy** | Pickup location matches listing; communicate changes immediately |
| **Identification** | Verify customer identity or order code at handoff |
| **Wait time** | Minimize customer wait once arrived; staff ready at window start |
| **Missed pickup** | Follow stated policy — communicate via order status; do not discard without policy allowance |
| **Food condition at handoff** | Proper temperature; sealed packaging |

Chronic late pickups or missed windows degrade fulfillment reliability metrics and [Trust Score](trust-and-safety-standards.md#trust-score).

## Reviews and Reputation

Reviews are verified-purchase social proof — protected for integrity.

### Creator responsibilities

| Responsibility | Standard |

| **No retaliation** | Never punish customers for honest reviews |
| **No review solicitation fraud** | No incentives conditioned on star rating |

### Review integrity rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|

## Trust Score

Trust Score is a composite signal reflecting creator quality and reliability. It influences discovery ranking and platform confidence.

### Components

| Component | Weight direction | Notes |
|-----------|------------------|-------|
| **Verification status** | Hard gate | Unverified creators excluded from paid orders |
| **Compliance currency** | High | Expired docs reduce score to zero eligibility |
| **Review quality** | Medium–high | Average rating, volume, recency |
| **Fulfillment reliability** | High | On-time rate, cancellation rate, missed windows |
| **Dispute rate** | High (inverse) | Elevated disputes reduce score |
| **Incident history** | High (inverse) | Food safety incidents heavily penalized |
| **Listing completeness** | Medium | Allergen and fulfillment fields complete |
| **Photography quality** | Low–medium | Affects merchandising eligibility over time |

## Violations and Enforcement

Quality violations follow the platform [Trust enforcement ladder](../../product/marketplace-mechanics.md#trust-enforcement-ladder):

```
Education → Warning → Listing restriction → Order suspension → Account suspension → Permanent removal
```

### Violation categories

| Category | Examples | Typical starting point |
|----------|----------|------------------------|
| **Critical — food safety** | Allergen mislabeling, unsafe temperature, confirmed illness link | Immediate suspension |
| **Critical — fraud** | Stolen photos, fake verification docs, off-platform payment fraud | Immediate suspension → permanent removal |
| **High — reliability** | Pattern of missed windows, chronic cancellations | Warning → order suspension |
| **High — misrepresentation** | Menu does not match delivered food | Warning → listing restriction |
| **Medium — communication** | Harassment, off-platform solicitation | Warning → suspension |
| **Medium — compliance lapse** | Expired food handler cert | Auto-suspend listings after grace |
| **Low — quality** | Poor photography, incomplete descriptions | Education → listing restriction |

Full enforcement framework: [Trust & Safety Standards — Enforcement Ladder](trust-and-safety-standards.md#enforcement-ladder).

### Creator notifications

Enforcement actions are communicated via Creator OS with:

- Clear reason code
- Evidence summary where applicable
- Remediation steps
- Appeal path — see below

## Suspensions

Suspensions protect customers while issues are investigated or remediated.

### Suspension types

| Type | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Listing restriction** | Cannot publish new or edited listings; existing may remain or pause per policy |
| **Order suspension** | Cannot accept new orders; fulfill existing orders unless safety issue |
| **Account suspension** | Full platform access restricted; payouts may be held |
| **Permanent removal** | Account terminated; no re-application without executive approval |

### Payout holds

Payment may be held during investigation of fraud, food-safety, or high-value disputes — [Payment Service](../../engineering/services/payment-service.md) · [Trust Service](../../engineering/services/trust-service.md).

### Reinstatement

Reinstatement requires:

1. Root cause remediated
2. Re-verification complete if kitchen/compliance affected
3. Acknowledgment of standards violation
4. Trust & Safety approval

## Appeals

Creators may appeal enforcement actions they believe are incorrect or disproportionate.

### Appeal process

| Step | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **1. Submit appeal** | Within 14 days of enforcement action via Creator OS compliance center |
| **2. Provide evidence** | Documentation, photos, order records, corrective actions taken |
| **3. Independent review** | Reviewer not involved in original decision (when staffing permits) |
| **4. Decision** | Uphold, modify, or reverse — within 5 business days |
| **5. Final escalation** | Trust lead review for unresolved critical cases |

### Appeal standards

| Outcome | When applied |
|---------|--------------|
| **Reverse** | Clear error, new evidence exonerates creator |
| **Modify** | Penalty disproportionate to violation severity |
| **Uphold** | Evidence supports original decision |
| **Deny frivolous** | Pattern of appeals without new evidence |

Appeals for permanent removal require senior Trust & Safety review. Appeal metrics tracked via [Verification appeal overturn rate](../../product/success-metrics-overview.md#verification-integrity).

Verification appeals follow [Trust Verification Flow](../../pages/flows/trust-verification-flow.md). Content moderation appeals: [Trust & Safety Standards — Appeals](trust-and-safety-standards.md#appeals).

## Continuous Improvement

Quality is not a one-time certification — it is ongoing operational discipline.

### Creator improvement practices

| Practice | Benefit |
|----------|---------|
| **Review analytics** | Identify recurring complaints — [Analytics](../../pages/creator/analytics.md) |
| **Order post-mortems** | After disputes or cancellations, document what changed |
| **Seasonal menu planning** | Align capacity and lead times with demand |
| **Staff training** | Food handler certification current; allergen protocols documented |
| **Equipment maintenance** | Calibrate thermometers; service refrigeration |
| **Community standards** | Stay current with platform policy updates |

### Platform support for improvement

| Resource | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Creator Success outreach | At-risk creator coaching when metrics decline |
| Compliance reminders | Proactive renewal notifications — [Creator Compliance](../../pages/creator/compliance.md) |
| Education content | Non-punitive guidance when listings frequently flagged |
| Trust Score transparency | Dashboard shows which components need attention (internal metrics) |

### Feedback loop

```
Customer reviews + dispute data + fulfillment metrics
    → Trust Score update
    → Discovery eligibility change
    → Creator Success intervention (if at-risk)
    → Creator remediation
    → Metric recovery
```
