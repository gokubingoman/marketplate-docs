# Chef Success Guide

> Operational playbook for growing a trusted, profitable food business on Marketplate — actionable tactics with metrics.

**Status:** Active  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last updated:** 2026-07-03  
**Audience:** Verified creators post-launch — independent chefs, bakers, meal prep operators, and related personas

## Purpose

Launching on Marketplate is the beginning, not the finish. This guide covers what separates creators who build durable businesses from those who stall after the first order wave.

For pre-launch preparation, see [Chef Welcome Package](chef-welcome-package.md) and [Chef Onboarding Checklist](chef-onboarding-checklist.md). For platform rules and trust mechanics, see [Marketplace Mechanics](../../product/marketplace-mechanics.md).

## Success Framework

Marketplate measures creator success across four dimensions. Optimize all four — not just revenue.

| Dimension | Key question | Primary metric |
|-----------|--------------|----------------|
| **Retention** | Do customers come back? | Repeat customer rate (30-day) |
| **Reliability** | Do you deliver what you promise? | Order completion rate · on-time ready rate |
| **Reputation** | Do customers trust you publicly? | Average review score · review response rate |
| **Economics** | Is the business sustainable? | GMV per active creator · AOV · net margin |

→ Metric definitions: [Success Metrics — Creator Metrics](../../product/success-metrics-overview.md#creator-metrics)

## Repeat Customers

Repeat purchase is the primary demand-side health signal. A creator with strong first-order volume but zero repeats has a product-market fit problem — not a marketing problem.

### Why repeats matter

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Lower acquisition cost | Returning customers skip discovery friction |
| Predictable production | Weekly regulars enable accurate batch planning |
| Trust Score | Repeat purchase signals feed discovery ranking |
| Resilience | Repeat base absorbs slow weeks |

### Tactics

| Tactic | Action | Target |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **Anchor day** | Same pickup day weekly (e.g., "Thursday meal prep") | ≥ 40% of orders from returning customers within 90 days |
| **Signature item** | Keep 1–2 always-available items alongside rotating specials | Signature item in ≥ 60% of repeat orders |
| **Reorder nudge** | Message customers post-fulfillment with next menu preview | Open rate tracked in [Messages](../../pages/creator/messages.md) |
| **Notification opt-in** | Encourage favorites; publish menu updates on consistent schedule | Follower → order conversion ≥ 10% |
| **Consistency audit** | Same portion, packaging, and quality every order | Zero "different from last time" reviews |

### Measurement

Check **Repeat vs. new customers** in [Analytics](../../pages/creator/analytics.md) weekly.

| Benchmark | Interpretation |
|-----------|----------------|
| < 20% repeat (30-day) | Quality, scheduling, or communication issue — investigate reviews |
| 20–40% repeat | Healthy early stage — optimize anchor day and messaging |
| > 40% repeat | Strong product-market fit — focus on capacity and AOV |

## Reviews

Reviews are verified-purchase only. They compound into Trust Score and storefront conversion. You cannot buy, remove, or suppress them.

### Review system rules

- Prompts send after order completed
- Public responses visible on [Reviews page](../../pages/creator/reviews.md)
- Flag policy violations (harassment, non-verified purchase) for moderation — not legitimate criticism
- Rating distribution displayed on storefront

### Response playbook

| Rating | Response template | Timeline |
|--------|-------------------|----------|
| **5 stars** | Thank by name; reference specific dish; mention next availability | Within 48 hours |
| **4 stars** | Thank; ask what would make it 5 (optional); note any fix | Within 48 hours |
| **3 stars** | Acknowledge; explain corrective action; invite direct message | Within 24 hours |
| **1–2 stars** | Acknowledge without defensiveness; offer resolution path; escalate internally if food safety concern | Within 12 hours |

### Proactive review generation

Do not ask for fake reviews. Do earn them:

1. **Packaging insert** — Small thank-you card with storefront link (no "leave 5 stars" — violates spirit of authentic reviews)
2. **Quality consistency** — Best review strategy is repeat-worthy food
3. **Issue resolution before review** — If something went wrong, fix it in [Messages](../../pages/creator/messages.md) before the review prompt fires

### Metrics

| Metric | Target | Where |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Average review score | ≥ 4.5 | [Reviews](../../pages/creator/reviews.md) |
| Review response rate | 100% within 48h for ≤ 3 stars | Reviews page |
| Review submission rate | Monitor trend | [Analytics](../../pages/creator/analytics.md) |

## Food Photography

Photography is your highest-ROI marketing asset on Marketplate. Discovery is photography-forward; quality affects conversion and, over time, discovery eligibility.

### Impact on conversion

| Quality level | Typical profile → cart impact |
|---------------|--------------------------------|
| No photo / stock | Baseline — avoid |
| Phone photo, poor light | −30–50% vs. optimized |
| Good natural-light photo | Baseline conversion |
| Professional or semi-pro | +20–40% vs. baseline |

### Optimization checklist

| Action | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Update seasonally** | Refresh photos when recipes change |
| **Match reality** | Portion size in photo = portion in bag |
| **Primary photo test** | A/B by swapping primary photo; compare 2-week conversion in Analytics |
| **Cross-section shots** | Show interior texture for breads, layers, fillings |
| **Consistent style** | Unified color and surface across catalog builds brand |

### Discovery eligibility

Below-minimum photography quality excludes items from featured collections. Platform photography scoring affects long-term discovery rank — not just launch.

→ Full standards: [Chef Welcome Package — Photography Guide](chef-welcome-package.md#photography-guide) · [Menu Item Editor](../../pages/creator/menu-item-editor.md)

## Trust

Trust is the product. Your verification status, operational reliability, and review history combine into **Trust Score** — a composite signal influencing discovery visibility and customer confidence.

### Trust Score components

| Component | How to improve |
|-----------|----------------|
| Verification currency | Renew docs before expiration; never miss compliance deadlines |
| Review quality | Maintain ≥ 4.5 average; respond professionally |
| Fulfillment reliability | Hit on-time ready rate ≥ 95% |
| Completion rate | Finish every confirmed order — target ≥ 98% |
| Dispute rate | Resolve issues directly; avoid platform-mediated losses |

### Trust badges

Customers see verification badges on your storefront and in search. Badges reflect **real-time status** — expired compliance removes badges immediately after grace period.

Do not claim verification you have not earned. Do not produce food in unverified locations — even for "just one order."

### Trust incidents to avoid

| Incident | Consequence |
|----------|-------------|
| Allergen mislabeling | Immediate suspension pending investigation |
| Expired compliance | Listing suspension after grace |
| Chronic late orders | Discovery ranking reduction |
| Creator-initiated cancellations | Reliability metric impact + customer trust loss |

→ Trust model: [Marketplace Mechanics — Trust Model](../../product/marketplace-mechanics.md#trust-model) · [Glossary — Trust Score](../../company/glossary.md#trust-score)

## Reduce Refunds

Refunds erode margin and Trust Score. Most are preventable with clear expectations and operational discipline.

### Root causes and fixes

| Cause | Fix | Metric to watch |
|-------|-----|-----------------|
| **Missed pickup window** | Clear pickup instructions; reminder message 2 hours before window | Missed pickup rate |
| **Quality mismatch** | Photo accuracy; consistent recipes; taste test every batch | "Not as expected" reviews |
| **Late orders** | Realistic capacity limits; update status if delayed | On-time ready rate |
| **Wrong order** | Label every bag with name + order number; check against [Order Detail](../../pages/creator/order-detail.md) | Wrong-item disputes |
| **Allergen reaction** | Complete allergen disclosure; never cross-contaminate flagged items | Trust incidents (zero tolerance) |
| **Policy confusion** | Clear cancellation/refund policy pre-checkout | Refund request rate |

### Refund request rate target

| Stage | Target |
|-------|--------|
| First 50 orders | < 5% refund request rate |
| Established (100+ orders) | < 2% refund request rate |

Platform target for order completion rate: **≥ 98%**.

### When refunds happen

Issue promptly when warranted. Disputes you lose impact metrics more than voluntary refunds you initiate with empathy. Document what went wrong and fix the process — not just the order.

## Average Order Value (AOV)

AOV growth increases revenue without proportional production overhead.

### Tactics

| Tactic | Implementation | Expected AOV lift |
|--------|----------------|-------------------|
| **Bundles** | "Family dinner for 4" vs. individual items | +25–40% |
| **Add-ons** | Side, dessert, drink as optional variant | +15–25% |
| **Minimum for delivery** | $35 minimum delivery order | Filters low-margin delivery |
| **Weekly subscription-style menu** | Pre-set box at fixed price | Stabilizes AOV |
| **Starting-at pricing** | Base + premium protein option | +10–20% |
| **Deposit items** | Catering deposits for high-ticket events | Captures large AOV events |

Configure in [Menu Item Editor](../../pages/creator/menu-item-editor.md) and [Catalog](../../pages/creator/catalog.md).

### Measurement

Track **AOV trend** in [Analytics](../../pages/creator/analytics.md) by week. Compare periods after bundle launches.

| Benchmark | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| AOV flat or declining | Introduce bundle; review pricing against food cost |
| AOV rising, orders flat | Bundles working — maintain quality at higher ticket |
| AOV rising, orders declining | Price may exceed perceived value — test smaller entry item |

## Menus

Menu strategy balances variety (discovery interest) with operational simplicity (batch efficiency).

### Menu architecture

| Layer | Purpose | Count |
|-------|---------|-------|
| **Anchors** | Always available; build repeat habit | 1–2 items |
| **Rotating** | Weekly or seasonal specials; drive return visits | 2–4 items |
| **Add-ons** | Increase AOV without new production line | 2–3 items |
| **Limited drops** | Create urgency without fake scarcity | 1 item, scheduled |

### Menu rotation cadence

| Persona | Recommended cadence |
|---------|---------------------|
| Meal prep | Weekly menu publish (same day each week) |
| Baker | Weekly or bi-weekly drop with cut-off |
| Independent chef | Bi-weekly rotation; anchor dish always on |
| Food truck | Daily or event-based via [Availability](../../pages/creator/availability.md) |
| Caterer | Event-driven; custom quote items |

### Sold-out discipline

Mark sold out immediately when capacity hits zero. Overselling creates food waste, refunds, and broken trust. System enforces capacity at checkout — your job is accurate limits, not manual counting.

### Catalog breadth metric

Median active SKUs per active creator is monitored platform-wide. Too few = limited conversion. Too many = operational chaos.

**Sweet spot:** 4–8 active items for most solo operators; 10–15 for meal prep with batch production.

## Pricing

You control pricing. Marketplate does not force discounts. Sustainable pricing protects your business and signals quality to trust-seeking customers.

### Quarterly pricing review

Every 90 days:

1. Calculate current food cost per top 3 items
2. Compare to listed price — target **≥ 30% gross margin** after food + packaging (before platform fee)
3. Adjust prices or portion sizes where margin erodes
4. Review competitor positioning — not race-to-bottom, but sanity check

### Pricing psychology for trust buyers

Trust-seeking customers accept premium pricing when:

- Verification badges and reviews justify it
- Photography and story communicate craft
- Consistency proves reliability over time

Underpricing signals low quality and attracts one-time bargain hunters who do not repeat.

### Delivery pricing

If you deliver, price for transport time and packaging:

```
Delivery price ≥ pickup price + (transport time × hourly rate) + packaging premium
```

Or enforce minimum order value instead of separate delivery fee — simpler for customers.

→ Foundation: [Chef Welcome Package — Pricing Recommendations](chef-welcome-package.md#pricing-recommendations)

## Customer Communication

You own the customer relationship. Marketplate facilitates messaging and order updates — use both proactively.

### Communication channels

| Channel | Use for |
|---------|---------|
| **Order status updates** | Confirmed → In production → Ready (automatic + manual override) |
| **[Messages](../../pages/creator/messages.md)** | Menu previews, delay notifications, post-order thank-you |
| **Review responses** | Public accountability and trust building |

### Response time targets

| Message type | Target response |
|--------------|-----------------|
| Pre-order question | < 4 hours |
| Active order issue | < 1 hour |
| Post-order follow-up | < 24 hours |

### Message templates

**Menu preview (weekly):**
> This week's menu is live on my Marketplate storefront — pickup Saturday 10–12. [Link]. Same favorites plus [new item]. Order by Thursday 6 PM.

**Delay notification:**
> Your order is running approximately [X] minutes behind schedule. It will be ready by [time]. Thank you for your patience.

**Post-fulfillment thank-you:**
> Thank you for your order today. Next week's menu drops [day] — hope to see you again.

### Tone

Warm, precise, human — per [Voice and Tone](../../brand/voice-and-tone.md). Name the customer. Name the dish. Give specific times, not "soon."

## Scale Your Kitchen

Growth without operational collapse requires capacity planning before demand exceeds supply.

### Capacity planning

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Capacity utilization > 85% consistently | Raise prices, add capacity limit, or expand production days |
| Waitlist demand (sold out in < 24h) | Add second pickup window or increase daily cap |
| Quality complaints increase | Reduce capacity — do not scale broken operations |
| Creator-initiated cancellations rise | Overcommitted — tighten limits |

Track **capacity utilization** in [Analytics](../../pages/creator/analytics.md): orders placed ÷ capacity available.

### Scaling stages

| Stage | Orders/week | Focus |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| **Solo proof** | 1–20 | Consistency, reviews, repeat rate |
| **Solo scale** | 20–50 | Batch optimization, capacity limits, packaging systems |
| **Team assist** | 50–100 | Prep delegation, standardized recipes, label systems |
| **Commercial transition** | 100+ | Commercial kitchen tenancy, team permissions (future), delivery partners |

### Batch production

| Practice | Benefit |
|----------|---------|
| Standardized recipes with weights | Consistent quality at volume |
| Prep day vs. pickup day separation | Reduces day-of chaos |
| Assembly line packaging | Faster handoff, fewer errors |
| Label printer with order details | Eliminates wrong-order disputes |

### When to expand kitchen type

If home/cottage kitchen caps your volume or category eligibility, transition to commercial shared kitchen. Update kitchen verification in [Compliance](../../pages/creator/compliance.md) before producing in the new location.

## Analytics

[Analytics](../../pages/creator/analytics.md) answers: *Is my business growing? Who comes back? What's working?*

### Weekly review ritual (15 minutes)

Every Monday, check:

| Metric | Question | Action if off-track |
|--------|----------|---------------------|
| **Revenue (7d vs. prior 7d)** | Growing or flat? | Share storefront; review pricing |
| **Order count** | Volume trend? | Check availability windows |
| **Repeat customer %** | Retention healthy? | See [Repeat Customers](#repeat-customers) |
| **AOV** | Ticket size trend? | See [AOV](#average-order-value-aov) |
| **Top items** | What's selling? | Promote winners; retire losers |
| **On-time ready rate** | Reliable? | Adjust capacity or lead times |
| **Average rating (30d)** | Quality signal? | Read recent reviews; respond |

### Monthly review (30 minutes)

| Metric | Benchmark | Source |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| GMV per month | Month-over-month growth | Analytics |
| Creator retention | Orders in M and M+1 | Analytics |
| Refund request rate | < 2% at scale | Payouts adjustments |
| Profile views → orders | Conversion trend | Analytics |
| Compliance currency | 100% valid docs | Compliance |

### Drill-down paths

Analytics links to actionable pages — use them:

- Low traffic → [Storefront Settings](../../pages/creator/storefront-settings.md)
- Low conversion → [Catalog](../../pages/creator/catalog.md) photos and pricing
- Low repeat → [Messages](../../pages/creator/messages.md) and menu cadence
- Late orders → [Availability](../../pages/creator/availability.md) capacity
- Rating drop → [Reviews](../../pages/creator/reviews.md)

### Export

Export CSV monthly for tax preparation and margin analysis. Cross-reference with [Payouts](../../pages/creator/payouts.md) net earnings.

## 90-Day Success Milestones

| Day | Milestone | Success indicator |
|-----|-----------|-------------------|
| **0** | Verified and published | Storefront live with ≥ 1 item |
| **7** | First 5 orders completed | Order completion rate 100% |
| **14** | Profile complete; catalog ≥ 3 items | Profile completeness score high |
| **30** | First repeat customers | Repeat rate ≥ 15% |
| **30** | Review foundation | ≥ 5 reviews; average ≥ 4.5 |
| **60** | Operational rhythm | Anchor day established; capacity predictable |
| **60** | AOV optimized | Bundle or add-on live |
| **90** | Sustainable business signal | ≥ 20 orders/month; repeat rate ≥ 25%; completion rate ≥ 98% |

Creators without a live listing at day 14 are at-risk — Marketplate may reach out with creator success support.

## Common Failure Patterns

| Pattern | Symptom | Fix |
|---------|---------|-----|
| **Launch and ghost** | No menu updates for 30+ days | Weekly cadence; even small rotation |
| **Overpromise capacity** | Late orders, cancellations | Reduce daily cap by 20% |
| **Photo neglect** | Views but no cart adds | Reshoot top 3 items |
| **Review ignore** | Unanswered negative reviews | Respond within 24h |
| **Compliance lapse** | Sudden listing suspension | Enable renewal reminders; calendar expirations |
| **Off-platform leakage** | Directing Marketplate customers to Venmo | Violates policy; undermines Trust Score |
