> Help Center · Analytics  
> Route: `/help/analytics`  
> Last updated: 2026-07-03

## Overview

Creator analytics show how customers discover your storefront, what they order, and how fulfillment and reviews perform over time. Use these metrics to improve menus, availability, and customer experience — not to chase vanity numbers.

This article is for **creators** using [Analytics](../../pages/creator/analytics.md) in Creator OS.

---

## Step-by-step guidance

### Step 1: Open your analytics dashboard

1. Sign in to Creator OS → **Analytics**.
2. Select date range — 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, or custom.
3. Overview shows orders, revenue, conversion rate, and average rating for the period.

[Screenshot: Analytics overview dashboard with KPI cards for orders, revenue, conversion rate, and average rating]

### Step 2: Review traffic and conversion

1. Open **Storefront traffic** — views, unique visitors, traffic sources (search, direct link, browse).
2. Compare views to orders for **conversion rate**. Low conversion with high views may indicate pricing, trust, or policy friction.
3. Track share link performance — which channels drive verified orders.

[Screenshot: Traffic chart with line graph over time and source breakdown pie chart labeled Search, Direct, Browse]

### Step 3: Analyze menu performance

1. Open **Menu performance** — top items by orders and revenue.
2. Identify sold-out patterns and underperforming items.
3. Cross-reference with [Catalog](../../pages/creator/catalog.md) — improve photos, descriptions, or pricing on low converters.

[Screenshot: Menu performance table with item name, orders, revenue, conversion rate, and trend arrow]

### Step 4: Monitor fulfillment metrics

1. Open **Fulfillment** — on-time ready rate, cancellation rate, dispute rate.
2. Chronic lateness affects discovery ranking inversely.
3. Creator-initiated cancellations impact reliability metrics — see [Marketplace Mechanics](../../product/marketplace-mechanics.md).

[Screenshot: Fulfillment metrics panel with on-time percentage, cancellation count, and dispute rate with benchmark comparison]

### Step 5: Act on insights

1. Adjust [Availability](../../pages/creator/availability.md) for capacity bottlenecks.
2. Update [Storefront](storefronts.md) story and photos if conversion lags.
3. Respond to review themes — see [Reviews](reviews.md).

[Screenshot: Analytics insight recommendation card suggesting "Increase Saturday pickup capacity based on sell-out trend"]

## Key metrics

| Metric | What it measures | Healthy direction |
|--------|------------------|-------------------|
| **Storefront views** | Discovery interest | Stable or growing with marketing |
| **Conversion rate** | Views → paid orders | Industry-appropriate; investigate drops |
| **Average order value** | Revenue per order | Reflects menu mix strategy |
| **On-time ready rate** | Ready within window | High — affects trust and ranking |
| **Cancellation rate** | Creator + customer cancels | Low creator-initiated cancels |
| **Dispute rate** | Orders requiring mediation | Low |
| **Review score** | Verified customer satisfaction | Stable or improving |

Metrics tie to [Creator Metrics](../../product/success-metrics-overview.md#creator-metrics) and [Trust Metrics](../../product/success-metrics-overview.md#trust-metrics).

## Common issues

| Issue | Likely cause | What to do |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| Analytics empty | New account; no orders yet | Complete [Storefronts](storefronts.md) launch; share link |
| Revenue mismatch vs. payouts | Payout schedule timing; fees | See [Payouts](../../pages/creator/payouts.md); compare date ranges |
| Traffic spike, no orders | Browsing only; trust or pricing barrier | Review storefront completeness and policies |
| Data delay | 24-hour processing window | Refresh next day; contact support if 48+ hours |
| Missing traffic source | Direct/referrer not captured | Encourage use of tracked share links |

## FAQs

### How often is data updated?

Most metrics refresh within 24 hours. Real-time order counts may appear on dashboard separately from analytics aggregates.

### Can I export data?

Export to CSV available for orders and revenue reports (feature availability by tier). Use for accounting and menu planning.

### Does analytics include cancelled orders?

Revenue metrics typically exclude fully refunded orders. Cancellation counts appear in fulfillment section.

### How does analytics affect search ranking?

Quality signals — review score, completion rate, dispute rate — influence ranking alongside verification and relevance. See [Marketplace Mechanics — Discovery](../../product/marketplace-mechanics.md#discovery).

## Escalation guidance

Contact support for:

- Analytics data missing 48+ hours with confirmed orders
- Revenue figures inconsistent with payout records after reconciliation attempt
- Suspected tracking bug on share links

**Support category:** General inquiry (creator)  
**Expected response:** Within 2 business days  
**Include:** Date range, metric name, expected vs. displayed values, order numbers for discrepancy examples
