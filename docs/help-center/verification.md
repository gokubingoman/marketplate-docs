> Help Center · Verification  
> Route: `/help/verification`

## Overview

Every creator who accepts paid orders on Marketplate completes verification before going live. Verification confirms identity, production location, and jurisdiction compliance — the foundation of customer trust. Unverified creators cannot publish paid listings.

This article is for **creators** applying to sell on Marketplate. Customers learning about badges should read [Trust & verification badges](trust.md). For the full onboarding path, see [Creator Onboarding](../onboarding/README.md).

---

## Step-by-step guidance

### Step 1: Complete identity verification

1. After [creator signup](../../pages/auth/creator-signup.md), open the verification dashboard.
2. Submit government ID, business registration (if applicable), tax identity, and contact verification.
3. Entity type is captured — sole proprietor, LLC, or partnership.
4. Our team reviews submissions within **2 business days**. AI assists document extraction; **humans approve** final status.

[Screenshot: Identity verification form with document upload zones for ID, business registration, and progress checklist]

### Step 2: Complete kitchen verification

1. Document your production location — home kitchen (cottage), commercial shared kitchen, dedicated facility, commissary, or food truck with commissary linkage.
2. Provide address confirmation, facility registration, inspection records where available, and photos of the production environment.
3. Every menu item must link to a verified production location before listing goes live.

[Screenshot: Kitchen verification wizard showing kitchen type selector, address fields, photo upload, and facility registration upload]

### Step 3: Submit compliance documents

1. Upload jurisdiction-specific documents: food handler certificate, business license, cottage food registration, liability insurance where required.
2. Compliance rules vary by state and locality — especially cottage food categories and sales caps.
3. Set renewal reminders; expired documents trigger listing suspension after the grace period.

[Screenshot: Compliance center with document list, expiration dates, status badges, and upload buttons]

### Step 4: Pass review and go live

1. Verification status moves: Submitted → In review → Approved (or Needs action).
2. If **Needs action**, review feedback, resubmit corrected documents, and await re-review.
3. Once all layers pass, publish your storefront and accept paid orders.

[Screenshot: Verification status dashboard with Identity, Kitchen, and Compliance rows showing green approved badges]

## Verification layers

| Layer | Confirms | Customer-visible outcome |
|-------|----------|-------------------------|
| **Identity** | Real person or legal entity authorized to operate | Verified Creator badge |
| **Kitchen** | Food produced in documented, approved environment | Verified Kitchen badge; location transparency |
| **Compliance** | Licenses, permits, food safety requirements for jurisdiction | Category eligibility; compliance status |

Kitchen verification is **not** a health inspection substitute. Marketplate verifies documentation and consistency; government authorities retain regulatory authority.

## Common issues

| Issue | Likely cause | What to do |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| Verification delayed past 2 business days | High volume or incomplete submission | Check for "Needs action" requests; ensure all fields complete |
| Kitchen rejected | Address mismatch, insufficient documentation | Re-read feedback; submit updated photos or registration |
| Compliance expired | Certificate past renewal date | Upload renewed document before grace period ends |
| Cannot publish paid listings | One or more layers incomplete | Complete all verification steps; drafts only until approved |
| Multi-tenant kitchen | Shared commercial space | Commercial kitchen verified once; link your permitted bay/schedule |

## FAQs

### How long does verification take?

Identity and kitchen review typically complete within 2 business days per submission. Resubmissions after "Needs action" restart review from receipt of corrected documents.

### Can I sell while verification is pending?

You may save drafts — menu items, storefront content — but cannot accept paid orders until fully verified. This protects customers per [Marketplace Mechanics](../../product/marketplace-mechanics.md).

### What happens when compliance documents expire?

Proactive reminders before expiration. After the grace period, listings suspend automatically — not silent continuation. Renew and resubmit to restore.

### Do I need to re-verify if I move kitchens?

Yes. Material kitchen changes require re-verification. Update production location before accepting orders from the new site.

## Escalation guidance

Contact support for verification issues:

- No response after 5 business days with complete submission
- Rejection reason unclear or you believe documentation was misread
- Urgent go-live date with complete submission pending review

**Support category:** Creator verification  
**Expected response:** Within 2 business days  
**Include:** Creator account email, verification layer (identity/kitchen/compliance), submission date, specific question

Do not send full ID numbers in support messages — reference submission date and document type only.
