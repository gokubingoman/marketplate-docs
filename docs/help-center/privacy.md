> Route: `/help/privacy`

## Overview

Marketplate collects data to operate the marketplace, process orders, verify creators, and improve the platform. We minimize collection to what is necessary and give you controls over preferences and account data. Full legal terms appear in privacy policy documents in [`legal/`](../../legal/) *(Phase 5)*.

This article explains what data we collect, how it is used, your choices, and data retention in plain language.

---

## Step-by-step guidance

### Step 1: Understand what we collect

| Data type | Examples | Purpose |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| **Account** | Name, email, phone | Authentication, order communication |
| **Order** | Items, fulfillment, payment last four | Transaction processing, support |
| **Location** | Delivery address, search area | Fulfillment, discovery |
| **Preferences** | Dietary flags, notification settings | Personalization, safety highlighting |
| **Creator verification** | ID, kitchen docs, compliance | Trust verification — restricted access |
| **Usage** | Pages viewed, search queries | Product improvement, analytics |
| **Reviews** | Rating, text, verified purchase link | Reputation system |

[Screenshot: Account settings Privacy section with data summary cards and "Download my data" link]

### Step 2: Manage your preferences

1. **Notifications** — [Notifications](notifications.md)
2. **Dietary preferences** — [Account](account.md)
3. **Marketing opt-out** — Account settings → Privacy → Marketing preferences
4. **Location** — Browser/app permission; search works without precise location using manual area entry

[Screenshot: Marketing opt-out toggles with clear labels for email, push, and creator follow updates]

### Step 3: Download your data

1. Account settings → **Privacy & data** → **Download my data**.
2. Request generates an export within the timeframe stated in privacy policy (typically 30 days or less).
3. Export includes profile, orders, messages, and review history in machine-readable format.

[Screenshot: Data export request confirmation with expected delivery date and email notification notice]

### Step 4: Delete your account

1. Account settings → **Delete account**.
2. Review consequences: order history access ends; active orders must complete first.
3. Confirm deletion. Grace period applies before permanent removal — see legal policy for duration.
4. Some records retained where legally required (tax, dispute, trust investigations).

[Screenshot: Delete account flow step 2 listing data retained vs. deleted with legal retention note]

## How data is shared

| Recipient | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| **Creators** | Fulfill your orders — name, contact, order details, notes |
| **Payment processor** | Process charges — tokenized payment data |
| **Service providers** | Hosting, email, analytics — under contract |
| **Legal authorities** | When required by law or valid legal process |

We do not sell personal information. Creator verification documents are not shared with customers beyond badge status and appropriate location transparency.

## Common issues

| Issue | Likely cause | What to do |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| Data export delayed | Large account; processing queue | Wait stated timeframe; contact support if exceeded |
| Cannot delete account | Active orders | Complete or cancel orders first |
| Creator has unexpected data | Shared for order fulfillment | Expected for orders placed; report misuse via Trust & safety |
| Location used unexpectedly | Search or delivery feature | Revoke browser location; enter area manually |

## FAQs

### Do creators see my full payment details?

No. Creators see order contents and fulfillment contact info — not full card numbers.

### Is my allergy information shared?

Dietary preferences and order notes are visible to the creator for orders you place. See [Food safety & allergens](food-safety.md).

### How long are messages kept?

Retained for dispute resolution period defined in privacy policy, then deleted or anonymized per retention schedule.

### What cookies does Marketplate use?

Essential cookies for authentication and cart. Analytics cookies per consent banner where required. Details in cookie policy (`legal/` Phase 5).

## Escalation guidance

Contact support for:

- Data export not received after stated timeframe
- Account deletion stuck in pending state
- Suspected unauthorized data access or creator misuse of customer data

**Support category:** Account or Trust & safety (misuse)  
**Expected response:** Within 24 hours (4 hours for unauthorized access)  
**Include:** Account email, request type, dates, order numbers if relevant

For formal privacy rights requests (CCPA, GDPR where applicable), use the privacy request form linked in Account settings when available, or support with subject "Privacy rights request."
