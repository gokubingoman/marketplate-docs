# Pickup

> Help Center · Pickup  
> Route: `/help/pickup`  
> Last updated: 2026-07-03

## Overview

Pickup is the most common fulfillment type on Marketplate — you select a scheduled window, the creator prepares fresh to that window, and you collect at the listed location. Pickup windows, addresses, and no-show policies are set by creators and shown before checkout.

This article covers selecting pickup times, finding the location, pickup codes, and missed windows.

---

## Step-by-step guidance

### Step 1: Select a pickup window

1. On the creator's storefront, add items to cart.
2. At checkout, view available **pickup windows** — date and time range (e.g., Saturday 11:00–11:30 AM).
3. Select a window that fits your schedule. System enforces capacity — unavailable windows are not selectable.
4. Lead times apply for batch and custom items — minimum days shown before checkout.

[Screenshot: Checkout pickup window selector with calendar, available slots highlighted, and sold-out slots grayed]

### Step 2: Review pickup location

1. Pickup address or general location displays at checkout and on [Order Detail](../../pages/customer/order-detail.md).
2. Full address may appear post-purchase or at pickup per creator privacy settings — check confirmation email.
3. Note parking, entrance, and special instructions in the order confirmation.

[Screenshot: Order confirmation with pickup address, map link, window time, and parking instructions]

### Step 3: Collect your order

1. Arrive within your scheduled window — not early unless the creator accepts early pickup via message.
2. When status is **Ready**, present your order number or **pickup code** if provided.
3. Verify items before leaving. Report issues immediately via [Messaging](messaging.md).

[Screenshot: Ready-for-pickup order detail with large pickup code, address, and "Get directions" link]

### Step 4: Same-day and ASAP pickup

Food trucks, pop-ups, and limited-inventory creators may offer same-day or ASAP pickup:

1. Real-time inventory — items sell out without backorder.
2. Windows may be shorter or rolling. Check storefront for **Open now** status.
3. Arrive promptly — same-day inventory does not guarantee hold beyond window.

[Screenshot: Food truck storefront with "Open now" badge, live location pin, and ASAP pickup option]

## Common issues

| Issue | Likely cause | What to do |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| Missed pickup window | Arrived late | Creator no-show policy applies — often no refund; food may be discarded |
| Order not ready at window start | Production delay | Message creator; wait reasonable time; see [Orders](orders.md) |
| Cannot find pickup location | Unclear instructions | Message creator; use map link on Order Detail |
| Wrong items at pickup | Fulfillment error | Inspect before leaving; message creator with photo |
| Need to change pickup window | Schedule conflict | Message creator before production; may require cancel/reorder |

## FAQs

### How early can I arrive?

Message the creator if you need early pickup. Standard policy: arrive within the scheduled window. Early arrival does not guarantee readiness.

### What happens if I miss my window?

Creators disclose no-show policies at checkout. Typical policy: orders not picked up within the window may be discarded without refund. Perishable food cannot be held indefinitely.

### Is the pickup address the verified kitchen?

Pickup may occur at the verified kitchen, a commercial kitchen bay, pop-up location, or food truck spot — all linked to creator fulfillment settings. Verified Kitchen badge confirms documented production; pickup point may differ for customer convenience.

### Can someone else pick up my order?

Yes, if you provide them the order number or pickup code. Ensure they know the window and location.

## Escalation guidance

Message creator first for not-ready orders, location confusion, or wrong items at pickup.

Contact support when:

- Creator unresponsive and order was not provided during window
- Dispute over no-show policy application — policy unclear vs. checkout disclosure
- Food safety concern with picked-up order

**Support category:** Order issue (fulfillment) or Food safety (quality/safety)  
**Expected response:** Within 24 hours (4 hours for food safety)  
**Include:** Order number, pickup window, arrival time, photos, creator message summary
