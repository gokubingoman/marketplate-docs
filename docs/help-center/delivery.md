# Delivery

> Help Center · Delivery  
> Route: `/help/delivery`  
> Last updated: 2026-07-03

## Overview

Some creators deliver orders within defined zones using their own staff or coordinated partners. Delivery availability, fees, and timing are set by each creator and shown before checkout. Marketplate enforces that you cannot complete checkout without a valid delivery option for your address.

This article covers delivery zones, tracking, fees, and issue resolution. For pickup-based orders, see [Pickup](pickup.md).

---

## Step-by-step guidance

### Step 1: Confirm delivery availability

1. On the [Creator storefront](../../pages/customer/creator-storefront.md), check the fulfillment badge — **Delivery** with zone description.
2. Enter or select your address at checkout. Addresses outside the zone cannot proceed.
3. Review delivery fee (if any), estimated arrival window, and minimum order requirements.

[Screenshot: Storefront fulfillment section showing delivery zone map or radius, fee, and minimum order]

### Step 2: Place a delivery order

1. Add items to [Cart](../../pages/customer/cart.md) and proceed to [Checkout](../../pages/customer/checkout.md).
2. Select **Delivery** and confirm your address is within zone — validation runs at checkout.
3. Add delivery instructions (gate code, leave at door, etc.) in order notes.
4. Complete payment. Order status: Confirmed → In production → Ready → In fulfillment → Completed.

[Screenshot: Checkout delivery section with address field, zone confirmation checkmark, delivery window, and instructions textarea]

### Step 3: Track your delivery

1. Open [Order Detail](../../pages/customer/order-detail.md) for status updates.
2. When status is **In fulfillment**, follow creator-provided tracking or contact instructions.
3. Partner/coordinated delivery tracking integrations are a future phase — v1 relies on creator updates and messaging.

[Screenshot: Order detail In fulfillment state with estimated arrival window and "Message creator" button]

### Step 4: Receive your order

1. Be available during the delivery window unless instructions specify otherwise.
2. Verify items on receipt. Report missing or wrong items immediately via [Messaging](messaging.md).
3. Order completes after handoff confirmation or auto-complete per policy.

[Screenshot: Completed delivery order with photo proof of delivery thumbnail if provided by creator]

## Delivery models

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Creator managed** | Creator or staff delivers within defined zone |
| **Partner coordinated** | Handoff to delivery partner (tracking future) |
| **Catering / event** | Scheduled delivery to event location — see checkout terms |

Perishable nationwide shipping is not a default v1 model. Creators ship non-perishables only where legally permitted.

## Common issues

| Issue | Likely cause | What to do |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| Address not in delivery zone | Outside creator radius | Choose pickup if available; try another creator |
| Delivery late | Production or traffic delay | Message creator; see [Orders](orders.md) |
| Wrong address delivered | Incorrect address entered | Verify saved address; contact creator immediately |
| Items cold or damaged | Transit time or packaging | Message creator with photos; request refund per policy |
| Delivery fee unexpected | Creator-set fee disclosed at cart | Review cart before payment — no hidden fees at checkout |

## FAQs

### Who delivers my order?

The creator or their designated delivery staff/partner — not Marketplate. The creator is merchant of record. Verification confirms identity and kitchen, not delivery driving.

### Is delivery fee separate from platform service fee?

Yes. Delivery fees (if charged) go to the creator's fulfillment costs. Platform service fee is separate and shown on cart summary. See [Payments & fees](payments.md).

### Can I schedule delivery for later?

Creators set available windows — same-day, scheduled slots, or event dates. Available windows appear at checkout; past windows cannot be selected.

### What if no one is home?

Follow instructions in order notes. Creators set policies for failed delivery — redelivery fee or no refund may apply if disclosed at checkout.

## Escalation guidance

Message creator first for late, missing, or wrong delivery on active orders.

Contact support when:

- Creator unresponsive 24+ hours during delivery window
- Complete non-delivery with no communication
- Safety concern with delivery handoff

**Support category:** Order issue  
**Expected response:** Within 24 hours  
**Include:** Order number, address, expected vs. actual delivery time, creator contact attempts, photos
