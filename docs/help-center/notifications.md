## Overview

Marketplate sends notifications to keep you informed about orders, messages, and account activity — without unnecessary noise. You control email, push, and SMS preferences in [Account settings](../../pages/customer/account-settings.md). Creators receive additional order and verification alerts in Creator OS.

This article covers notification types, preference management, and troubleshooting delivery issues.

---

## Step-by-step guidance

### Step 1: Open notification settings

1. Sign in → **Account settings** → **Notifications**.
2. View channels: Email, Push (mobile app), SMS (where available).
3. Toggle categories independently per channel.

[Screenshot: Notification settings page with channel columns and category rows with toggle switches]

### Step 2: Configure customer notifications

| Category | Typical alerts | Recommended |
|----------|----------------|-------------|
| **Order updates** | Confirmed, In production, Ready, Completed | On — essential for pickup/delivery |
| **Messages** | New creator message on active order | On |
| **Marketing** | Creator you follow posted new menu | Off unless desired |
| **Account** | Security alerts, password changes | On (email) |
| **Reviews** | Review prompt after completion | Email on |

[Screenshot: Order updates section expanded showing example notification previews for Ready and Completed states]

### Step 3: Configure creator notifications (Creator OS)

1. Creator OS → Settings → **Notifications**.
2. Enable **New order**, **Order cancellation**, **Verification status**, **Compliance expiration**, and **Customer message** alerts.
3. Set quiet hours if available — orders still appear in dashboard regardless.

[Screenshot: Creator notification settings with New order push enabled and compliance expiration email enabled]

### Step 4: Follow creators (optional)

1. Follow a creator from their storefront to receive menu and availability updates.
2. Follow notifications are marketing-category — opt-in only.
3. Unfollow anytime from storefront or notification settings.

[Screenshot: Storefront "Follow" button with subtitle "Get notified when new menu drops"]

## Common issues

| Issue | Likely cause | What to do |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| Not receiving order emails | Category disabled; spam filter | Check notification settings and spam; add noreply@marketplate.com to contacts |
| Push not working (mobile) | OS permissions denied | Enable notifications in device Settings → Marketplate app |
| Too many emails | All categories enabled | Disable Marketing; keep Order updates only |
| SMS not arriving | Phone unverified; carrier block | Verify phone in account; check SMS toggle |
| Duplicate notifications | Email + push both on | Expected if both enabled — disable one channel |

## FAQs

### Can I turn off all notifications?

Order-critical notifications may still send for active orders regardless of marketing preferences — legal and fulfillment requirements. You can minimize to email-only order updates.

### Will I miss Ready for pickup if push is off?

Enable email for Order updates or check [Order Detail](../../pages/customer/order-detail.md) manually. Missing Ready notification does not extend pickup window.

### Do creators get notified of bad reviews?

Creators receive new review notifications. Rating threshold alerts may be configurable in Creator OS.

### How do I stop texts from a creator I followed?

Unfollow the creator or disable Marketing SMS in notification settings. Follow is opt-in.

## Escalation guidance

Contact support if:

- Order notifications absent for 48+ hours with confirmed active order and settings correct
- Unsubscribe link not working
- Suspected unauthorized notification (account compromise)

**Support category:** Account (general) or Account security (suspicious alerts)  
**Expected response:** Within 24 hours (4 hours for security)  
**Include:** Account email, notification type missing, sample order number, device and app version
