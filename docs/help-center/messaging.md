# Messaging

> Help Center · Messaging  
> Route: `/help/messaging`  
> Last updated: 2026-07-03

## Overview

Marketplate messaging connects customers and creators around orders — pickup questions, dietary clarifications, timing updates, and professional dispute resolution. Messages are order-scoped where possible, logged for dispute mediation, and moderated for harassment and policy violations.

This article covers starting conversations, messaging etiquette, response expectations, and when to escalate beyond messaging.

---

## Step-by-step guidance

### Step 1: Start a conversation (customers)

1. Open [Order Detail](../../pages/customer/order-detail.md) for an active or recent order.
2. Select **Message creator**.
3. Write a clear, specific message — include order number (auto-attached), question, and relevant context.
4. For severe allergies, message **before production starts** in addition to checkout notes — see [Food safety & allergens](food-safety.md).

[Screenshot: Customer order detail with "Message creator" button and thread showing order context header]

### Step 2: Respond to customers (creators)

1. Open [Messages](../../pages/creator/messages.md) in Creator OS or from order notification.
2. Select the thread — order context displays at top.
3. Reply within your stated response window (recommended: within 4 hours during business hours).
4. Keep communication professional; move payment or refund disputes to platform policy paths.

[Screenshot: Creator messages inbox with unread badge, order number, customer first name, and reply composer]

### Step 3: Use messaging appropriately

**Good uses:**
- Pickup location clarification
- Allergy cross-contact questions
- Timing updates or delays
- Wrong item correction coordination
- Custom order detail confirmation

**Not for messaging:**
- Payment charge disputes → [Payments](payments.md) / support
- Refund demands bypassing stated policy → [Refunds](refunds.md) then support
- Harassment, threats, or off-platform solicitation

[Screenshot: Message thread with examples of appropriate pickup clarification vs. flagged inappropriate content banner]

### Step 4: When the creator does not respond

1. Wait a reasonable window — 24 hours for non-urgent; sooner for same-day pickup issues.
2. Check order status — creator may be in production.
3. After 24 hours without response on an active order, contact [support](contact-support.md) with message attempt summary.

## Common issues

| Issue | Likely cause | What to do |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| Cannot message creator | Order too old or messaging closed post-completion | Use support for post-completion issues; reviews for feedback |
| Messages not delivering | Notification settings; app background | Check [Notifications](notifications.md); refresh thread |
| Creator asks for off-platform payment | Policy violation | Decline; report via Trust & safety |
| Inappropriate message content | Harassment or spam | Report in-thread; block if available; contact support |
| Language barrier | Creator primary language differs | Be patient; use clear simple language; support can assist |

## FAQs

### Are messages private?

Messages are visible to the customer, creator, and Marketplate support when mediating disputes. Do not share sensitive payment details (full card numbers) in messages.

### Can I message before ordering?

Pre-order inquiries may be available on some storefronts (future). For v1, place order with notes or message immediately after confirmation for urgent dietary questions.

### How long are messages retained?

Per [Privacy](privacy.md) retention policy. Threads remain accessible for active orders and a defined period post-completion for dispute resolution.

### Can creators message marketing promotions?

Unsolicited marketing violates [Community guidelines](community-guidelines.md). Customers may opt into creator follow notifications separately — see [Notifications](notifications.md).

## Escalation guidance

Contact support when:

- Creator unresponsive 24+ hours on active order with time-sensitive issue
- Harassment, threats, or off-platform payment requests in messages
- Dispute unresolved after good-faith messaging exchange (48+ hours)

**Support category:** Order issue (timing/fulfillment) or Trust & safety (conduct)  
**Expected response:** Within 24 hours  
**Include:** Order number, message dates/times, summary of ask and response (screenshots helpful)

Platform encourages creator ↔ customer resolution first per [Marketplace Mechanics — Disputes](../../product/marketplace-mechanics.md#disputes).
