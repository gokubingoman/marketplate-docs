# Platform Policies

> Help Center · Platform policies  
> Route: `/help/platform-policies`  
> Last updated: 2026-07-03

## Overview

Platform policies define the rules Marketplate, creators, and customers operate under — seller obligations, buyer responsibilities, enforcement, disputes, and prohibited conduct. Policies displayed at checkout are binding for that transaction. Full legal text lives in [`legal/`](../../legal/) *(Phase 5)*.

This article summarizes key policies and where to find details. When summary and legal text differ, legal text governs.

---

## Step-by-step guidance

### Step 1: Understand roles

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| **Customer** | Accurate info, allergen review, timely pickup/delivery cooperation, honest reviews |
| **Creator (seller)** | Accurate menus, verified kitchen production, compliance docs current, policy adherence |
| **Marketplate** | Verification, payments facilitation, dispute mediation, enforcement |

Creator is **merchant of record**. Marketplate is marketplace facilitator — see [Marketplace Mechanics](../../product/marketplace-mechanics.md).

[Screenshot: Checkout policy acknowledgment section with links to cancellation, refund, and allergen policies]

### Step 2: Review policies before purchase

At checkout you acknowledge:

1. **Cancellation policy** — window and refund terms for this creator
2. **Allergen acknowledgment** — you reviewed creator-provided allergen info
3. **Terms of service** — platform rules

Deep links: [Refunds & cancellations](refunds.md) (`#cancellations`), [Food safety & allergens](food-safety.md) (`#allergens`), [Payments & fees](payments.md) (`#fees`).

[Screenshot: Checkout checkbox group with expandable policy summaries and "Read full policy" links]

### Step 3: Creator seller obligations

Creators agree to seller terms including:

- Maintain current [Verification](verification.md) and compliance
- Accurate [Menus & catalog](menus.md) with allergen disclosure
- Honor fulfillment windows and stated policies
- Respond to customer messages within reasonable time
- Accept platform-mediated dispute resolution

Violations follow the [enforcement ladder](../../product/marketplace-mechanics.md#trust-enforcement-ladder).

[Screenshot: Creator compliance dashboard with policy acknowledgment date and seller terms link]

### Step 4: Dispute resolution process

1. **Direct resolution** — Customer and creator via [Messaging](messaging.md) encouraged first.
2. **Platform mediation** — Either party contacts support when unresolved.
3. **Outcomes** — Refund, partial refund, credit, or no action — logged in audit trail.
4. **Appeals** — Submit within window stated in resolution email.

[Screenshot: Dispute timeline diagram showing Direct → Mediation → Outcome → Appeal stages]

### Step 5: Prohibited activities (summary)

- Unverified paid sales
- Allergen mislabeling
- Fake reviews or review extortion
- Off-platform payment for platform orders
- Harassment, discrimination, fraud
- Prohibited food categories per jurisdiction
- Pay-to-hide negative reviews

Full list in terms of service and [Community guidelines](community-guidelines.md).

## FAQs

### Can creators set their own refund policies?

Within platform minimums. Stricter creator policies must be disclosed pre-checkout. Platform force-cancel always full refund for safety/compliance.

### Does Marketplate arbitrate all disputes?

Mediation available when direct resolution fails. Outcomes logged. Not all disputes result in refund — depends on facts and policies acknowledged.

### How do I appeal account suspension?

Instructions in suspension notification. Submit appeal via support with Trust & safety category within stated window.

### Are pop-up and catering orders covered by same policies?

Same framework; deposit and event-specific terms disclosed at checkout for custom fulfillment models.

## Escalation guidance

Contact support for:

- Policy clarification affecting active dispute
- Appeal of enforcement action or dispute outcome
- Report creator operating outside verified/compliance status

**Support category:** Trust & safety (enforcement) or Payment & refund (disputes)  
**Expected response:** 24–48 hours depending on category  
**Include:** Order number, policy version date if known, enforcement case ID, appeal grounds with evidence

Legal requests from attorneys: route via legal contact in terms of service when published.
