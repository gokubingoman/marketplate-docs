# Manage Your Account

> Help Center · Account  
> Route: `/help/account`  
> Last updated: 2026-07-03

## Overview

Your Marketplate account stores your profile, saved addresses, notification preferences, dietary settings, and order history. You can update most settings yourself in [Account settings](../../pages/customer/account-settings.md) without contacting support.

This article covers sign-in, profile updates, saved addresses, dietary preferences, and account deletion. For password security, see [Security](security.md). For notification controls, see [Notifications](notifications.md).

---

## Step-by-step guidance

### Step 1: Access account settings

1. Sign in to Marketplate.
2. Select your account icon in the top navigation.
3. Choose **Account settings** to open your profile, addresses, notifications, and privacy controls.

[Screenshot: Account dropdown menu with "Account settings," "Order history," and "Sign out" options]

### Step 2: Update your profile

1. In Account settings, open the **Profile** section.
2. Edit your display name, phone number, and profile photo if supported.
3. Select **Save changes**. Your email address change may require re-verification.

[Screenshot: Profile section with name, email, phone fields and Save button]

### Step 3: Manage saved addresses

1. Open the **Addresses** section.
2. Select **Add address** to save a new delivery or billing address.
3. Edit or remove existing addresses. Set a default address for faster checkout.
4. Addresses must be within a creator's delivery zone to be selectable at checkout — zone validation happens on the creator's storefront.

[Screenshot: Address list with default badge, edit and delete icons, and "Add address" button]

### Step 4: Set dietary preferences

1. Open **Dietary preferences** in Account settings.
2. Select allergens or dietary tags you want flagged during browsing (e.g., peanuts, gluten-free).
3. These preferences highlight relevant menu items but do not replace per-item allergen review at checkout — see [Food safety & allergens](food-safety.md).

[Screenshot: Dietary preferences checklist with allergen tags and save confirmation toast]

### Step 5: Sign out or delete your account

**Sign out:** Account menu → **Sign out**. Your order history remains accessible when you sign back in.

**Delete account:**
1. Open **Privacy & data** in Account settings.
2. Select **Delete account** and confirm.
3. Active orders must be completed or cancelled before deletion. Deletion is permanent after the grace period stated in our privacy policy — see [Privacy](privacy.md).

[Screenshot: Delete account confirmation dialog with consequences listed and confirm/cancel buttons]

## Common issues

| Issue | Likely cause | What to do |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| Cannot change email | Pending verification on new address | Check inbox for verification link; try again after 15 minutes |
| Address not available at checkout | Outside creator delivery zone | Choose pickup or a different creator; verify address is saved correctly |
| Account locked after failed sign-in | Multiple incorrect password attempts | Wait 30 minutes or reset password via [Security](security.md) |
| Cannot delete account | Active or in-production orders | Complete or cancel open orders first |

## FAQs

### Can I have multiple accounts?

One account per email address. Household members should use separate accounts if they need separate order histories and payment methods.

### Will deleting my account remove my reviews?

Reviews from verified purchases may remain anonymized per our [Privacy policy](privacy.md) and [Reviews](reviews.md) guidelines. Personal identifiers are removed from your profile.

### How do I update my payment methods?

Payment methods are managed at checkout or in your wallet section if available. Saved cards are tokenized — Marketplate does not store full card numbers. See [Payments & fees](payments.md).

### I ordered as a guest — can I link orders to an account?

Contact support with your order number and the email used at checkout. We can associate past guest orders when verification succeeds.

## Escalation guidance

Contact support for account issues you cannot resolve in settings:

- Email change blocked after verification attempts
- Suspected unauthorized account access — use **Account security** category (4-hour response)
- Account deletion request with active dispute or refund in progress
- Guest order linking with missing confirmation email

**Support category:** Account  
**Expected response:** Within 24 hours (4 hours for security)  
**Include:** Account email, description of issue, order numbers if relevant

Do not share your password in support messages.
