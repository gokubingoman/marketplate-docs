## Overview

Your storefront is your public presence on Marketplate — story, menu, verification badges, reviews, fulfillment options, and policies. A complete storefront converts discovery traffic into orders and sets accurate expectations before checkout.

This article is for **creators** managing their verified storefront. Customers browsing storefronts should see [Getting started](getting-started.md) and [Trust & verification badges](trust.md).

---

## Step-by-step guidance

### Step 1: Complete verification first

1. Finish [Identity, kitchen, and compliance verification](verification.md) before publishing paid listings.
2. Unverified creators cannot accept paid orders — draft mode only.
3. Verification badges display automatically when approved.

[Screenshot: Storefront settings with verification checklist showing all three layers approved before "Publish" enabled]

### Step 2: Build your profile

1. Open [Storefront settings](../../pages/creator/storefront-settings.md) in Creator OS.
2. Add cover photo, profile photo, creator story, and cuisine tags.
3. Write in your voice — customers buy from people, not catalog entries. Follow [Voice and Tone](../../brand/voice-and-tone.md): warm, precise, trustworthy.
4. Set business name as customers will see it — consistent with verification documents.

[Screenshot: Storefront editor with cover image upload, story textarea, cuisine tags, and live preview panel]

### Step 3: Configure fulfillment and policies

1. Set pickup windows, delivery zones, lead times, and capacity in [Availability](../../pages/creator/availability.md).
2. Define cancellation, refund, and no-show policies — displayed at checkout.
3. Configure handoff instructions: pickup codes, truck location updates, catering setup notes.

[Screenshot: Fulfillment configuration with pickup schedule, delivery radius slider, and policy text fields]

### Step 4: Publish and share

1. Preview your [Customer-facing storefront](../../pages/customer/creator-storefront.md).
2. Select **Publish** when menu and fulfillment are complete.
3. Share your storefront link on social media, email, and print — tracked in [Analytics](analytics.md).

[Screenshot: Published storefront with share link button, QR code option, and social share icons]

### Step 5: Maintain your storefront

1. Update hours for holidays and blackouts.
2. Mark sold-out items promptly — overselling is a platform defect.
3. Respond to reviews and messages within stated windows.
4. Renew compliance documents before expiration to avoid suspension.

[Screenshot: Storefront dashboard alert banner for expiring compliance document with renew CTA]

## Common issues

| Issue | Likely cause | What to do |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| Publish button disabled | Verification incomplete or menu empty | Complete [Verification](verification.md); add at least one active menu item |
| Storefront not in search | New listing indexing delay; compliance gap | Allow 24 hours; confirm verification and compliance current |
| Low conversion, high views | Trust gaps, pricing, or unclear policies | Add photos, complete story, clarify fulfillment; check [Analytics](analytics.md) |
| Badges not showing | Verification pending or expired compliance | Check verification dashboard |
| Wrong fulfillment options showing | Availability misconfiguration | Update [Availability](../../pages/creator/availability.md) and blackout dates |

## FAQs

### Can I customize my storefront URL?

Custom slug URLs are available per creator settings (e.g., `marketplate.com/c/marias-kitchen`). Choose once — changes may break shared links.

### Do I need professional photography?

Photography standards affect discovery eligibility over time. Clear, well-lit food photos outperform stock images. See platform quality standards in [`docs/standards/`](../standards/).

### Can I run promotions or discount codes?

Promotion features roll out by phase. Check Creator OS for available merchandising tools. Promotions cannot hide verification status.

### What if I operate multiple locations?

Each production location requires kitchen verification. Link menu items to correct verified kitchens. Food trucks need commissary linkage.

## Escalation guidance

Contact support for storefront issues:

- Verification approved but publish blocked (technical)
- Storefront incorrectly suspended
- Search/discovery indexing failure after 48 hours verified live

**Support category:** Creator verification or General inquiry  
**Expected response:** Within 2 business days  
**Include:** Creator account email, storefront URL, screenshot of issue, verification status

For menu or compliance content rejection, include specific item SKU and rejection reason from dashboard.
