# Getting Started with Marketplate

> Help Center · Getting started  
> Route: `/help/getting-started`  
> Last updated: 2026-07-03

## Overview

Marketplate connects you with verified local food creators — independent chefs, bakers, and caterers who prepare food in documented kitchens you can trust. Unlike anonymous delivery apps, you see who makes your food, where it is produced, and what other verified customers say before you order.

This guide covers account creation, discovering creators, placing your first order, and what to expect after checkout. For how verification works, see [Trust & verification badges](trust.md).

---

## Step-by-step guidance

### Step 1: Create your account

1. Go to [marketplate.com](https://marketplate.com) or open the Marketplate app.
2. Select **Sign up** and enter your email and password, or continue with a supported sign-in provider.
3. Confirm your email address from the verification link we send you.
4. Optionally add a saved address and dietary preferences in [Account settings](../../pages/customer/account-settings.md).

[Screenshot: Sign-up form with email, password, and "Create account" button on a clean white background]

### Step 2: Discover verified creators

1. From [Home](../../pages/customer/home.md), browse featured creators or select **Browse** to explore collections.
2. Use [Search](../../pages/customer/search.md) to filter by cuisine, dietary needs, fulfillment type (pickup or delivery), and verification status.
3. Open a [Creator storefront](../../pages/customer/creator-storefront.md) to read their story, view verification badges, check reviews, and browse their menu.

[Screenshot: Search results showing creator cards with verification badge, star rating, fulfillment type icon, and distance]

### Step 3: Review trust signals before ordering

Before adding items to your cart, confirm:

- **Verified Identity** and **Verified Kitchen** badges on the creator profile
- Allergen and ingredient disclosures on each [menu item](../../pages/customer/menu-item-detail.md)
- Fulfillment method, pickup window or delivery zone, and lead time
- Cancellation and refund policy summary on the storefront

[Screenshot: Creator storefront header with verification badges, rating summary, and fulfillment options tabs]

### Step 4: Place your first order

1. Add items to your [Cart](../../pages/customer/cart.md).
2. Select a valid pickup window or delivery option — the system will not allow checkout without viable fulfillment.
3. Review the fee breakdown, tax estimate, and total on [Checkout](../../pages/customer/checkout.md).
4. Acknowledge allergen information if flagged items are in your order.
5. Enter payment details and confirm your order.

[Screenshot: Checkout trust strip showing verification badges, order summary, fee line items, and allergen acknowledgment checkbox]

### Step 5: Track and complete your order

1. After confirmation, view your order on [Order Detail](../../pages/customer/order-detail.md).
2. Watch status updates: Confirmed → In production → Ready → Completed.
3. Follow pickup or delivery instructions from the creator.
4. Leave a review after your order is fulfilled — reviews require a verified purchase.

[Screenshot: Order detail page with status timeline, pickup window, address, and "Get help with this order" link]

## Common issues

| Issue | Likely cause | What to do |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| No creators appear in search | Supply gap in your area or filters too narrow | Widen search radius; remove dietary filters; try Browse |
| Cannot complete checkout | No valid pickup window or delivery slot selected | Return to cart; choose an available fulfillment option |
| Verification badges missing on a listing | Creator not yet verified — cannot accept paid orders | Choose a verified creator; unverified creators cannot go live |
| Email confirmation not received | Spam filter or typo in email address | Check spam; request new confirmation from login page |

## FAQs

### Do I need an account to browse?

No. You can browse creators, menus, and reviews without signing in. An account is required to place orders, save addresses, and contact creators about active orders.

### How is Marketplate different from food delivery apps?

Marketplate is a verified supply marketplace. Every paid listing comes from a creator who completed identity and kitchen verification. You buy directly from the creator — they are the merchant of record. See [Trust & verification badges](trust.md) for details.

### Can I order from multiple creators at once?

Each creator operates their own storefront with separate checkout. You will complete one order per creator. Your [Order history](../../pages/customer/order-history.md) shows all orders in one place.

### What payment methods are accepted?

Major credit and debit cards are supported at checkout. See [Payments & fees](payments.md) for full details.

## Escalation guidance

Most getting-started questions are answered in the articles above. Contact support if:

- You cannot create an account after multiple attempts
- You believe a creator is operating without proper verification
- You encounter a technical error that blocks checkout on a verified storefront

**Support category:** General inquiry  
**Expected response:** Within 24 hours  
**Include:** Screenshot of the error, browser or app version, creator name (if applicable)

For food safety or allergic reaction concerns, use the **Food safety** category — see [Contact support](contact-support.md) for priority routing.
