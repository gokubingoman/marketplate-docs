## Overview

Reviews on Marketplate come from verified purchases only — customers who actually received the order. They provide authentic reputation signals for creators and accountability for quality. Reviews are moderated; fraud, harassment, and policy violations are removed with an appeals path.

This article covers leaving reviews, reading reviews credibly, creator responses, and reporting problematic content. For trust badge context, see [Trust & verification badges](trust.md).

---

## Step-by-step guidance

### Step 1: Leave a review (customers)

1. After your order reaches **Completed**, you receive a review prompt via email and in-app notification.
2. Open the prompt or navigate from [Order Detail](../../pages/customer/order-detail.md) → **Leave a review**.
3. Rate 1–5 stars, write optional text focused on food quality, fulfillment, and communication.
4. Submit — your review displays as a **Verified purchase**.

[Screenshot: Review submission form with star selector, text area, and "Verified purchase" badge preview]

### Step 2: Read reviews (customers)

1. On [Creator storefront](../../pages/customer/creator-storefront.md), scroll to the reviews section.
2. View aggregate rating distribution and recent reviews — recent reviews carry more display weight.
3. Read creator responses for context on how issues were handled.
4. Reviews reflect individual experiences — read multiple before deciding.

[Screenshot: Review list with star ratings, verified purchase badges, dates, and creator reply beneath a review]

### Step 3: Respond to reviews (creators)

1. Open [Reviews](../../pages/creator/reviews.md) in Creator OS.
2. Select a review → **Respond publicly**.
3. Keep responses professional — acknowledge feedback, explain resolution, avoid arguing.
4. Disputes about facts: respond calmly; escalate to support for policy violations in review text.

[Screenshot: Creator reviews dashboard with respond button, public reply composer, and review detail panel]

### Step 4: Report a problematic review

1. Select **Report** on any review (customers and creators).
2. Choose reason: fake, harassment, off-topic, policy violation, conflict of interest.
3. Trust & Safety reviews reports — outcomes include removal, edit request, or no action.

[Screenshot: Report review modal with reason dropdown and optional description field]

## Review rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Verified purchase only** | Must complete order to review |
| **Post-completion window** | Prompt after fulfillment; window defined in policy |
| **One review per order** | Edits allowed within window |
| **Moderation** | Fraud and harassment removed |
| **Creator response** | Public, professional replies encouraged |
| **No pay-to-remove** | Reviews never suppressed for payment |
| **No incentivized fake reviews** | Prohibited — account enforcement |

## Common issues

| Issue | Likely cause | What to do |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| No review prompt | Order not marked completed; outside window | Check Order Detail status; contact support if completed 7+ days ago |
| Review removed | Policy violation confirmed | Check email for reason; appeal if you disagree |
| Creator not responding | Optional for creators | Judge by other reviews; message creator directly about active issues |
| Suspected fake review | Policy violation | Report with specifics — platform verifies purchase linkage |
| Low rating after dispute | Legitimate feedback | Creators: respond professionally; customers: accurate experience is allowed |

## FAQs

### Can I edit or delete my review?

Edit within the post-completion window from Order Detail or review confirmation email. Deletion requests: contact support — reviews may be anonymized rather than deleted to preserve integrity.

### Do creators see reviews before I post?

No. Reviews publish upon submission (after automated checks). Creators are notified of new reviews.

### Can a creator refuse service because of a bad review?

Creators manage their own business relationships within platform policies. Retaliation or harassment violates [Community guidelines](community-guidelines.md) — report if experienced.

### Are negative reviews hidden?

No. Negative verified reviews display alongside positive. Ranking may weight recency and distribution — not payment to suppress.

## Escalation guidance

Contact support for:

- Review prompt missing on completed order after 7 days
- Review removed — request appeal with review ID
- Harassment or threats in review text or creator response
- Suspected coordinated fake reviews

**Support category:** Trust & safety (review reports) or General inquiry (technical prompt issues)  
**Expected response:** Within 24 hours  
**Include:** Order number, review date, screenshot of review, report reason

Creators disputing factual errors: respond publicly first; escalate only for policy violations or defamation meeting platform thresholds.
