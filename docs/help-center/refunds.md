# Refunds & Cancellations

> Help Center · Refunds  
> Route: `/help/refunds`  
> Last updated: 2026-07-03

## Overview

Cancellation and refund rules are disclosed before you pay at checkout and on the creator's storefront. Marketplate mediates disputes when you and the creator cannot agree. Refunds return to your original payment method in 5–10 business days after approval.

This article covers customer-initiated cancellation, creator-initiated cancellation, custom order deposits, and refund timing. Deep link anchor: `#cancellations` on the in-app Help page.

---

## Step-by-step guidance

### Step 1: Check if you can cancel

1. Open [Order Detail](../../pages/customer/order-detail.md) for the order.
2. If eligible, **Cancel order** appears with the cancellation deadline and policy summary.
3. Cancellation is typically available before production starts, within the creator's stated window. Custom orders and deposits may have different terms — disclosed at checkout.

[Screenshot: Cancel order button with inline policy text showing deadline, e.g., "Cancel by Thursday 6 PM for full refund"]

### Step 2: Cancel your order

1. Select **Cancel order** on Order Detail.
2. Review the refund amount — full refund within policy window; partial or no refund if outside window per creator policy.
3. Confirm cancellation. You receive email confirmation and refund status updates.

[Screenshot: Cancel confirmation modal with refund amount, policy summary, and Confirm/Cancel buttons]

### Step 3: Request a refund after fulfillment

If your order completed but did not meet expectations:

1. Message the creator via [Messaging](messaging.md) with specific details and photos if applicable.
2. Request a refund or replacement per their policy.
3. If unresolved after 48 hours, contact support with your conversation summary.

[Screenshot: Order detail "Get help with this order" link leading to support form with order pre-attached]

### Step 4: Track refund status

1. Refund status appears on Order Detail: Requested → Approved → Processed.
2. Funds return to your original payment method in **5–10 business days** after processing — timing depends on your bank.
3. Partial refunds show the adjusted amount and reason.

[Screenshot: Refund status badge on order detail with timeline and expected return date]

## Cancellation scenarios

| Scenario | Default outcome |
|----------|-----------------|
| **You cancel within policy window** | Full refund per creator/platform policy |
| **You cancel after window or production started** | Refund per creator policy — may be partial or none |
| **Creator cancels** | Full refund automatically; impacts creator reliability metrics |
| **Custom order / catering deposit** | Deposit forfeiture rules disclosed pre-payment |
| **Platform force-cancel** | Full refund — safety or compliance issue; creator under review |
| **Missed pickup (no-show)** | Typically no refund — see [Pickup](pickup.md) |

Policies are displayed pre-checkout per [Marketplace Mechanics](../../product/marketplace-mechanics.md#cancellations-and-refunds) and versioned in legal policy documents.

## Common issues

| Issue | Likely cause | What to do |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| No cancel button | Window closed or order in production | Message creator; contact support if exceptional circumstances |
| Refund approved but not on statement | Bank processing delay (5–10 business days) | Wait full window; contact support with refund confirmation |
| Partial refund unexpected | Policy allows partial outside window | Review checkout policy acknowledgment; dispute via support |
| Creator offered credit instead of refund | Creator discretion within policy | Accept or escalate to support for mediation |
| Deposit non-refundable | Disclosed catering/custom terms | Review checkout disclosure; contact support only if terms misrepresented |

## FAQs

### How long do refunds take?

After approval, 5–10 business days to your original payment method. Some banks post on the next billing cycle.

### Can I cancel a custom or catering order?

Custom orders often have stricter cancellation terms and deposit rules — all disclosed at checkout before payment. Read the policy checkbox acknowledgment on [Checkout](../../pages/customer/checkout.md).

### What if the creator cancels my order?

You receive a full refund automatically. You do not need to request it. Consider ordering from the creator again if the cancellation was due to capacity — they will notify you.

### Who decides refund disputes?

Encouraged first: direct resolution with the creator. Unresolved disputes: Marketplate Trust & Safety mediates with logged outcomes — refund, partial refund, credit, or no action.

## Escalation guidance

Contact support when:

- Creator unresponsive to refund request after 48 hours
- You believe checkout policy was misrepresented
- Refund approved but not received after 10 business days
- Platform-mediated dispute needed after creator contact

**Support category:** Payment & refund  
**Expected response:** Within 24–48 hours  
**Include:** Order number, cancellation/refund request date, creator communication summary, photos if quality issue

From [Order Detail](../../pages/customer/order-detail.md), use **Get help with this order** to attach context automatically.
