# Education Playbooks

> Seller education, buyer education, email sequences, support touchpoints, and communication standards.

## Purpose

Education converts activation into retention. Marketplate's education strategy teaches creators to **run trusted food businesses on-platform** and buyers to **purchase with confidence** — aligned with the four value dimensions in [Value Propositions](../../product/value-props.md).

This document defines curriculum structure, email playbooks, support touchpoints, and communication standards. External-facing creator guides live in [onboarding/](../onboarding/). Voice rules live in [Brand Voice & Tone](../../brand/voice-and-tone.md).

## Communication Standards

All CS, support, and lifecycle email communication must follow these standards.

### Voice attributes

| Attribute | Standard | Example |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| **Direct** | State facts; no filler | "Your kitchen verification is in review." |
| **Trust-forward** | Lead with verification, not promotions | "You're verified — customers can see your kitchen status." |
| **Specific** | One clear next action per message | "Publish your first listing to go live." |
| **Accountable** | Own platform issues; don't blame users | "We couldn't process your payout. We're investigating." |
| **Creator-named** | Reference the creator's business name | "Hi [Business Name] team" |

→ Full voice guide: [Brand Voice & Tone](../../brand/voice-and-tone.md)  
→ Support tone: [Customer Support section](../../brand/voice-and-tone.md#customer-support)

### Channel standards

| Channel | Use for | Response SLA | Notes |
|---------|---------|--------------|-------|
| **Email** | Lifecycle sequences, verification updates, education | T1: 24h · T2+: per intervention tier | Plain text preferred for transactional |
| **In-app** | Activation checklist, order alerts, compliance reminders | Real-time | Match dashboard context |
| **Push notification** | Order events, action required | Real-time | Action-oriented copy only |
| **Phone/video** | T3+ at-risk, high-GMV creators | Scheduled within SLA | Document outcome in CRM |
| **Chat (future)** | Support triage | Per support SLA | Escalate trust issues immediately |

### Prohibited patterns

| Pattern | Replace with |
|---------|--------------|
| "We apologize for any inconvenience" | Specific action + timeline |
| "Oops!" in payment or safety contexts | Calm, factual error explanation |
| "Powerful tools" / "Revolutionary platform" | Specific feature name: "production queue" |
| "Uber Eats for local food" | "Verified local food marketplace" |
| Discount-first re-engagement | Creator updates, trust reminders |
| Blame shifting to creator or buyer | Neutral policy explanation + next step |

## Seller Education

### Curriculum map

| Module | Stage | Format | Primary value dimension |
|--------|-------|--------|------------------------|
| **Welcome to verification** | Applicant | Email + in-app | Trust |
| **Verification resubmission** | Applicant (action required) | Email | Trust |
| **You're verified — go live** | Verified | Email + in-app checklist | Trust + Software |
| **First listing mastery** | Verified → Activated | Email + help article | Trust |
| **Availability & capacity** | Activated | Email + in-app | Software |
| **Share your storefront** | Activated | Email | Community |
| **First order fulfillment** | First order | In-app tooltip + email | Software |
| **Reviews & reputation** | Post-first-order | Email | Community + Trust |
| **Compliance maintenance** | Ongoing | Email (30/14/7-day reminders) | Trust |
| **Growth & optimization** | Productive | Email series | Economics + Software |

### Persona-specific education emphasis

| Persona | Priority modules | Skip/de-emphasize |
|---------|------------------|-------------------|
| [Independent Chef](../../product/personas.md#independent-chef) | Profile story, share link, allergy flags | Batch production (until volume warrants) |
| [Meal Prep Business](../../product/personas.md#meal-prep-business) | Cut-off times, capacity by week, nutrition fields | Custom order deposits |
| [Baker](../../product/personas.md#baker) | Lead times, allergen labeling, seasonal capacity | Event mode |
| [Caterer](../../product/personas.md#caterer) | Lead time by headcount, deposit policies | Location schedule |
| [Food Truck Operator](../../product/personas.md#food-truck-operator) | Location schedule, sell-out updates, pre-orders | Catering inquiry flow |
| [Cottage Food Operator](../../product/personas.md#cottage-food-operator) | Compliance checklist, category restrictions, sales caps | Delivery zone setup (if prohibited) |
| [Pop-Up Kitchen](../../product/personas.md#pop-up-kitchen) | Event listings, pre-pay, waitlist | Ongoing availability windows |
| [Commercial Kitchen Operator](../../product/personas.md#commercial-kitchen-operator) | Tenant linking, kitchen doc renewal | Individual kitchen upload (if facility verified) |

### Seller education topics by value dimension

#### Trust education

- Why verification converts browsers to buyers
- How to complete allergen and ingredient fields (mandatory, not optional)
- Compliance document renewal — consequences of expiration
- How reviews work (verified purchase only)

#### Software education

- Catalog manager and menu item editor walkthrough
- Production queue: Confirm → Produce → Ready → Complete
- Availability windows and capacity limits
- Customer messaging within order context
- Payout visibility and settlement timing

#### Community education

- How discovery works for verified creators
- Encouraging verified-purchase reviews (not incentivized fake reviews)
- Sharing storefront link vs. off-platform ordering

#### Economics education

- Transparent fee structure (when decided — no invented numbers)
- Pricing strategy: bundles, deposits, pre-order windows
- GMV growth without overcommitting capacity

## Buyer Education

### Curriculum map

| Module | Stage | Format | Primary value dimension |
|--------|-------|--------|------------------------|
| **Welcome — verified marketplace** | Registered | Email | Trust |
| **How verification protects you** | Registered / pre-first-order | Email + help article | Trust |
| **Reading a creator profile** | Pre-first-order | In-app on storefront | Trust |
| **Allergens & dietary filters** | Pre-checkout | Checkout module + help | Trust |
| **Fulfillment options explained** | Checkout | Inline helper text | Software |
| **Order tracking** | Post-purchase | Email + push | Software |
| **Reviews that matter** | Post-completion | Email prompt | Community |
| **Reorder & favorites** | Trust established | Email | Community + Software |
| **Dispute & refund path** | As needed | Help center | Trust |

### Buyer education principles

1. **Never assume buyers understand cottage food rules or kitchen types** — explain in plain language
2. **Allergen education is safety-critical** — link to standardized listing fields, not generic disclaimers
3. **Set fulfillment expectations before payment** — pickup windows, creator policies
4. **Platform support vs. creator responsibility** — clear boundary: creator fulfills; platform mediates disputes

→ Primary persona: [End Customer (Trust-Seeking Buyer)](../../product/personas.md#end-customer-trust-seeking-buyer)

## Email Playbooks

### Conventions

| Element | Standard |
|---------|----------|
| **Subject line** | Specific + action: "Publish your first listing — you're verified" |
| **Preview text** | Complement subject; don't repeat |
| **Body length** | ≤150 words for lifecycle; ≤250 for education |
| **CTA** | One primary button; verb-noun label |
| **Signature** | "— The Marketplate team" (not individual unless T3+ personal outreach) |
| **Footer** | Unsubscribe for marketing; transactional exempt with preference center |

### Creator email sequences

#### Sequence C1 — Application started, not submitted (Day 0, 3, 7)

| Email | Day | Subject | Body focus | CTA |
|-------|-----|---------|------------|-----|

| C1-B | 3 | Your application is waiting | Time estimate for review once submitted | Resume application |

## Support Touchpoints

### Support vs. CS boundary

| Function | Handles | Does not handle |
|----------|---------|-----------------|
| **Support** | Order issues, refunds, policy questions, account access | Proactive retention campaigns |
| **CS** | Activation, education, at-risk intervention, expansion | Real-time order troubleshooting |
| **Trust & Safety** | Verification, disputes with safety implications, suspensions | General "how do I" questions |

Handoff rule: If a support ticket reveals **at-risk creator** or **high-value repeat buyer at churn risk**, tag CS within 24 hours.

### Support touchpoint map

| Touchpoint | User | Trigger | Owner | SLA |
|------------|------|---------|-------|-----|
| Help center | Creator + Buyer | Self-serve | Content team | Always available |
| Email support | Creator + Buyer | Ticket submitted | Support | 4h first response (business hours) |
| In-app help | Creator + Buyer | Help link | Support | Same as email |
| Verification status inquiry | Creator | Waiting > SLA | Support → Trust | Transparent timeline |
| Order dispute | Buyer | Dispute opened | Support → Trust (if safety) | Per trust SLA |
| At-risk outreach | Creator | Health score | CS | Per intervention tier |
| High-GMV check-in | Creator | Quarterly | CSM | Scheduled |

### Support response templates (structure)

Follow [Customer Support tone](../../brand/voice-and-tone.md#customer-support):

| Situation | Structure |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Order issue** | Empathy → investigation commitment + timeline → next update point |
| **Verification delay** | Status → expected decision date → what creator can do while waiting |
| **Policy explanation** | Neutral policy statement → how it applies to this case → next step |
| **Escalation** | Acknowledge severity → name team escalating to → expected timeline |

**Avoid:** Blame shifting, passive voice, canned empathy without action.
