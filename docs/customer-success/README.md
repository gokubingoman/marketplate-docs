## Purpose

Customer Success at Marketplate is not generic SaaS account management. We enable **verified food businesses** to run durable operations and **trust-seeking buyers** to build repeat relationships with local creators. Success is measured by [Verified GMV](../../product/success-metrics-overview.md#north-star-metric) and its trust guardrails — not raw signups or vanity engagement.

This folder is the **internal operating system** for CS. Creator-facing guides live in [onboarding/](../onboarding/). Product strategy lives in [product/](../../product/). Flow specs live in [pages/flows/](../../pages/flows/).

## Core Principles

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Trust first** | Never accelerate activation by skipping verification or transparency requirements |
| **Persona-aware** | A cottage food operator and a meal prep business need different education — see [Personas](../../product/personas.md) |
| **Value-aligned outreach** | Lead with the value dimension that matters most per persona — see [Value Propositions](../../product/value-props.md) |
| **Proactive, not reactive** | Health scoring triggers intervention before churn, not after |
| **Creator owns the relationship** | CS enables creators to serve buyers; platform support mediates disputes, not daily order ops |
| **Quality over volume** | Prefer repeat purchase rate over one-time discount hunters |

## Team Scope & Handoffs

| Function | Owns | Hands off to |
|----------|------|--------------|
| **Creator Success** | Onboarding activation, catalog quality, operational readiness, creator retention | Trust & Safety for verification appeals; Support for order disputes |
| **Customer Experience** | Buyer education, post-purchase satisfaction, repeat purchase | Support for ticket resolution; Trust for safety incidents |
| **Trust & Safety** | Verification review, moderation, suspensions | CS for reactivation planning after resolution |
| **Support** | Ticket triage, order issues, policy explanation | CS for at-risk account intervention; Trust for escalations |
