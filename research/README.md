## Key Conclusions (Summary)

These conclusions are developed fully in the linked documents. Use them as orientation, not substitutes for the analysis.

1. **Marketplate is not in the food delivery category.** Uber Eats and DoorDash optimize restaurant logistics and platform-owned customer relationships. Marketplate optimizes creator trust, food-native operations, and creator-owned relationships. See [Positioning — Category Definition](../brand/positioning.md#category-definition).

2. **The real competition is fragmentation, not a single incumbent.** Creators today stitch together Instagram, Square, Shopify, spreadsheets, and DMs. Generic tools verify almost nothing; delivery apps exclude most creator types entirely. Marketplate's category — **verified creator infrastructure** — sits in a gap no incumbent fills. See [Market Context — The Market Gap](../company/market-context.md#the-market-gap).

3. **Trust is the wedge, OS depth is the moat.** Marketplace discovery alone is replicable. Identity + kitchen + compliance verification, food-native fulfillment workflows, and creator OS retention compound over time. See [Executive Summary — Competitive Advantage](../docs/internal/executive-summary.md#competitive-advantage).

4. **Market timing is favorable but geography-specific.** Cottage food law expansion, shared kitchen growth, and consumer skepticism of anonymous sellers create tailwinds — but SAM and SOM depend on launch market selection. `TODO(decision):` Quantify for founding geography. See [Market Analysis — TAM/SAM/SOM](market-analysis.md#tamsamsom-framework).

## Research Standards

- **Opinionated, not encyclopedic.** We state where Marketplate wins and where incumbents have durable advantages.
- **Category-correct comparisons.** Do not benchmark Marketplate against delivery app UX or restaurant POS feature checklists.
- **Decision markers.** Open quantification and strategic choices use `TODO(decision):` per [constitution standards](../company/constitution.md#quality-bar).
- **Living documents.** Update when launch geography is resolved, major competitors shift strategy, or new cottage food / creator-commerce entrants emerge.
